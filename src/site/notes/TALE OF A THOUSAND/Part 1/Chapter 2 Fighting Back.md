# Chapter 2: Fighting Back
You arrive back at the burning [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Locations/Town\|Town]], the air thick with smoke and the shouts of invading [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Raiders\|Raiders]].

"You are completely defenseless. A pathetic state to be in. I sense a weapon left inside [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Locations/Your House\|your house]], though there are plenty of other blunt objects scattered around this burning trash heap. Until you pick something up, stay out of sight. Try not to get slaughtered." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|Tome]]
## 🪓 Mechanics & Exploration: The Scavenge

*   **Stealth Phase:** The player must avoid the line of sight of the [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Raiders\|Raiders]]. 
*   **Pre-Combat Strike:** Like *Super Paper Mario*, once a weapon is equipped, striking an enemy in the overworld before they touch you will transition you into the arena with that enemy in a **weakened state**.
*   **Available Weapons:**
    *   [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Weapons/Branch\|Branch]] (Basic)
    *   [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Weapons/Frying Pan\|Frying Pan]] (Blunt)
    *   [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Weapons/Dagger\|Dagger]] (Fast)
    *   Eating Utensils Pack ([[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Weapons/Spoon\|Spoon]], [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Weapons/Fork\|Fork]], and [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Weapons/Knife\|Knife]])
    *   [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Easter Eggs/The Legendary Banana\|The Legendary Banana]] 🌟 *(Easter Egg)*
Upon picking up your first weapon, a surge of oppressive dark energy flows from the book, imbuing the item with a violet, smoky aura.
"Crude, but it will do for now. Now... let us find a stray dog to practice on." - [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|Tome]]
## ⚔️ First Encounter: The Basics of Fighting

*   **Encounter Style:** *Deltarune / Paper Mario* Arena style. If a Raider spots you, they give chase. Contact teleports the player into a dedicated combat Arena.

During the first battle, [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]] forces a tutorial on the [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Others/Basics Of Fighting\|Basics Of Fighting]].

The player defeats the Raider. The enemy collapses.

"You actually survived. Not bad for a weakling. Now... stand back. It is time for dinner." - [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|Tome]]

A horrific tearing sound echoes as the Tome forcibly tears the Raider's soul from their chest and devours it. A sudden, intoxicating rush of raw strength surges through your character's veins—accompanied by a dark, dangerous urge to feel it again. Your curse runes pulse with a violent dark glow.

"Feels good, doesn't it? That is the taste of power, human. With every soul you reap, our tether strengthens. In fact, you've just unlocked a fraction of my arcana: [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Spells/Dark Blast\|Dark Blast]]. Use it wisely." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]
## 🛡️ Second Encounter: Loot & Parrying
The player engages and defeats a second Raider.

"Every insect you crush will drop something useful—weapons, materials, coins, and experience points. But never forget that their souls belong to *Me*. Now, pick up that raider's discarded blade. It is heavy enough to **Parry**. Time it right in combat, and you will negate damage entirely. I can unlock more weapon features for you soon... but I require three more souls. Go on. Exterminate the rest of these pests." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]
## 🔑 3 Souls Later: Weapon Skills Unlocked
After harvesting three more souls, the area grows quiet.

"Excellently done. You've nearly cleansed your little nest of this infestation. As a reward for your diligence, I've unlocked your weapon's latent potential. Every tool of death carries a unique **Weapon Skill**—bashes, spins, thrusts... Press `<Weapon Ability Hotkey (E)>` during combat to unleash them." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

Suddenly, the crackle of burning wood is interrupted by a weak, agonized groan nearby.
*(Badly hurt, coughing)* "H-help... anyone..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

"What's that noise?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

You follow the sound through the smoke. Collapsed against a shattered stone wall, you find [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]. He is badly wounded, clutching his chest.

"Ow... Oh, hey `<user>`, What's up? `*Cough*`" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

"What happened?!?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"The raiders... they cornered me. I couldn't defend myself. They left me when they thought I was dead. And... honestly, I soon will be." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

The heavy footsteps of oncoming enemies echo nearby. A small squad of scavengers spots you.

"Oh, this is it for me" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

"I'll defend you, no matter what!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]
## ⚔️ The Defense of Peter
*   **Encounter:** An intense Arena battle against a wave of 3 [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Raiders\|Raiders]]. The player must defeat them to protect Peter's dying body.
The final raider falls. You turn back to Peter, who is fading fast.

"Thanks for defending me. But I'm really hurt, I won't make it." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

"Of course you will!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"Listen... I want you to have this..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

With the last of his strength, Peter presses a [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Others/PageStone\|glowing stone]] into your hands. The moment your fingers touch it, your curse marks flash in violent response.
"What is it?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"I think it's a valuable stone... I found it in a cave once. I've kept it ever since. I want you to have it." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

"The boy held onto a miracle... That is one of my torn pages. Place it inside of me. Now." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|Tome]]

You place the PageStone onto the book. A brand new, burnt page materializes within the Tome, revealing a list of high-tier locked spells—each displaying a heavy soul requirement.
"I want you to remember this... No matter where you go, or what you do... don't forget who you are inside." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

Peter's eyes gently close. His head slumps. He falls unconscious—drifting close to death.
## The Possession
"`<username>`... your friend. His soul... it is incredibly powerful. Untouched by malice. Pure energy." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

"You're not suggesting..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"I am telling you. Reap him." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

"I won't ever do that!!!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"Have you forgotten our little arrangement so quickly, human? I froze the poison in your veins, but I can easily let it thaw. Your life belongs to *me*. You signed the pact, and now it is time to pay up." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

"I don't care! I'd rather die than hurt Peter!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"He is dying anyway. Why let such a magnificent meal go to waste? 
# DO IT."
"- [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

"No!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

You try to cast the book aside, but your glowing curse marks flare up with blinding, agonizing violet light. Dark tendrils erupt from the book, wrapping violently around your arms and forcing your hands back toward Peter's chest.

"Once you begin, there is no going back. You belong to me now." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

Your character's body moves entirely against your will, reaching out toward Peter.
The screen cuts violently to absolute pitch-black.
In the darkness, a loud, horrifying sound plays: a soul being violently ripped apart and absorbed.

*(A long, suffocating moment of silence passes in the blackness...)*

The camera snaps back to the game world. Your character stands frozen over the empty pile of ash where Peter once lay. The Soul Counter on your UI clicks upward. The ambient music is completely gone—replaced only by the low, roaring crackle of the flames.

"Look at your hands, human. Do you feel it? The warmth? That is the flame of a pure soul. Don't let it go to waste. We have an infestation to finish cleansing." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

The player gains control. The character's idle animation has shifted—they stand taller, surrounded by a faint, pulsing violet aura. As you walk through the burning streets, any remaining minor Raiders scream and flee from you in terror. You don't have to chase them; the path naturally guides you to the village square.
## The Raid Boss Encounter

You step into the square. Standing over the ruins of the Town Hall is the monolithic commander of the attack: The [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Raid Boss\|Raid Boss]]. He turns.

"So, you're the brat who's been slaughtering my scouts? I'm going to paint these walls with your—" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Raid Boss\|Raid Boss]]

The Boss stops mid-sentence, stepping back as he senses the overwhelming, suffocating dark energy radiating from you. His aggressive grin fades into confusion.

"...What *are* you?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Raid Boss\|Raid Boss]]

"An apex predator, though he doesn't know it yet. Defeat this brute, partner. I can taste the raw power simmering inside him from here." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]
## ⚔️ BOSS BATTLE: Raid Boss

The Arena triggers. This is a high-stakes battle where you get to unleash your newly unlocked [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Spells/Dark Blast\|Dark Blast]] and practice your parrying against heavy, devastating attacks.

The Boss falls to his knees, defeated, before collapsing face-first into the dirt.
## 🗺️ The Bitter Departure

You stand amidst the ashes of your village. The fire reflects in your character's eyes. You look at the slain bodies, remembering what you just did to Peter... the realization of what you are becoming sinks in.

You turn to leave the ruins behind, but a glint of parchment catches your eye. You look down and spot a detailed tactical map tucked into the boss's armor.

"Take it. A hunter always maps out his next feeding ground." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

You pick up the map. The screen shifts to a hand-drawn map UI. Your burning village is crossed out in red. Several other locations across the continent are marked with dark runes. One coastal outpost catches your eye.

"The Pirate Docks..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"A lawless hive of scum, murderers, and thieves. Perfect. No one will miss them, and their souls will taste exquisite. Let us set sail, partner." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

Your character turns their back on the flaming ruins of their home and walks out into the dark forest, embarking on a grim journey.
