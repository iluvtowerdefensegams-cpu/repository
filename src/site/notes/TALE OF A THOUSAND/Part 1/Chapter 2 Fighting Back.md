# Chapter 2: Fighting Back
You arrive back at the burning [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Locations/Town\|Town]], the air thick with suffocating black smoke and the chaotic shouts of invading [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Raiders\|Raiders]].

"Look at you. Completely defenseless. A pathetic, helpless state to be in. I sense a weapon left inside [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Locations/Your House\|your house]], though there are plenty of crude, blunt objects scattered around this burning trash heap. Until you pick something up, stay out of sight. Try not to get slaughtered." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]
## 🪓 Mechanics & Exploration: The Scavenge
 * **Stealth Phase:** Navigate through the smoke-choked streets to avoid the line of sight of roaming [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Raiders\|Raiders]].
 * **Pre-Combat Strike:** Like *Super Paper Mario*, once a weapon is equipped, striking an enemy in the overworld before they spot you transitions into combat with the enemy in a **weakened state**.
 * **Available Weapons:**
   * [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Equipment/Weapons/Branch\|Branch]] (Basic)
   * [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Equipment/Weapons/Frying Pan\|Frying Pan]] (Blunt)
   * [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Equipment/Weapons/Dagger\|Dagger]] (Fast)
   * Eating Utensils Pack ([[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Equipment/Weapons/Spoon\|Spoon]], [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Equipment/Weapons/Fork\|Fork]], and [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Equipment/Weapons/Knife\|Knife]])
   * [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Easter Eggs/The Legendary Banana\|The Legendary Banana]] 🌟 *(Easter Egg)*
Upon picking up your first weapon, a subtle pulse of dark magic flows from the Tome into the item, imbuing it with a faint violet sheen.

"Crude, but it will suffice for now. Now... let us find a stray dog to practice on." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]
## ⚔️ First Encounter: The Basics of Fighting
 * **Encounter Style:** *Deltarune / Paper Mario* Arena style. If a Raider spots you, they give chase. Contact teleports the player into a dedicated combat Arena.
During the first battle, [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]] forces a brief tutorial on the [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Others/Basics Of Fighting\|Basics Of Fighting]].
The player defeats the Raider. The enemy collapses into the dirt.

"You actually survived. Not bad for a weakling. Now... stand back. It is time for dinner." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

A horrific, sickening tearing sound echoes through your head as the Tome forcibly tears the Raider's soul from their chest and devours it. A sudden, intoxicating surge of raw physical strength rushes through your veins—accompanied by a dark, dangerous thrill. Your curse marks pulse once.

"Feels good, doesn't it? That is the taste of power, human. With every soul you reap, our tether strengthens. In fact, you've just unlocked a fraction of my arcana: [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Spells/Dark Blast\|Dark Blast]]. Use it wisely." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]
## 🛡️ Second Encounter: Loot & Parrying
The player engages and defeats a second Raider.

"Every insect you crush will drop something useful—weapons, materials, coins, and experience points. But never forget that their souls belong to *Me*. Now, pick up that raider's discarded blade. It is heavy enough to **Parry**. Time it right in combat, and you will negate damage entirely. I can unlock more weapon features for you soon... but I require three more souls. Go on. Exterminate the rest of these pests." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]
## 🔑 3 Souls Later: Weapon Skills Unlocked
After harvesting three more souls, the immediate area grows quiet.

"Excellently done. You've nearly cleansed your little nest of this infestation. As a reward for your diligence, I've unlocked your weapon's latent potential. Every tool of death carries a unique **Weapon Skill**—bashes, spins, thrusts... Press `<Weapon (E) Ability Hotkey>` during combat to unleash them." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

Suddenly, the crackle of burning wood is interrupted by a weak, agonizing groan nearby.
*(Badly hurt, coughing on smoke)* "H-help... anyone..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

"What's that noise?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

You rush through the smoke toward the sound. Collapsed against a shattered stone wall, you find [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]. He is badly wounded, clutching his chest.

"Ow... Oh, hey `<username>`... What's up? *Cough*" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

"Peter?! What happened?!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"The raiders... they cornered me. I couldn't defend myself. They left me when they thought I was dead... And, honestly... I think I am..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

Heavy footsteps echo through the burning alleyway. A squad of three scavengers rounds the corner, spotting you both.

"Oh man... this is it for me..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

"I'll defend you, no matter what!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]
## ⚔️ The Defense of Peter
 * **Encounter:** An intense Arena battle against 3 [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Raiders\|Raiders]]. The player must defeat them to protect Peter's dying body.
The final raider falls. You turn back to Peter, who is fading fast, his breathing shallow and ragged.

"Thanks for defending me... But I'm really hurt... I won't make it..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

"Of course you will! Hold on!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"Listen... I want you to have this..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

With the absolute last of his strength, Peter presses a [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Others/PageStone\|glowing stone]] into your hand. The moment your fingers touch it, your curse marks flash in violent response.

"What is this?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"I think it's... a valuable stone... I found it in a cave once. Kept it as a lucky charm... I want you to have it..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

"The boy held onto a miracle... That is one of my torn pages. Place it inside of me. Now!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

You press the PageStone against the book's leather cover. It dissolves into the binding, and a brand new, burnt page materializes within the Tome, displaying a list of locked spells with massive soul requirements.

"I want you to remember this... No matter where you go... or what you do... don't forget who you are inside..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

Peter's eyes gently close. His hand falls limp onto the ash-covered floor. He slumps into total silence—drifting close to death.
## 🖤 The Forced Extraction
"`<username>`... your friend. His soul... it is incredibly powerful. Untouched by malice. Pure energy." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

"You're not suggesting..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"I am telling you. Reap him." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

"I won't EVER do that!!!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"Have you forgotten our little arrangement so quickly, human? I froze the poison in your veins, but I can easily let it thaw. Your life belongs to *Me*. You signed the pact, and now it is time to pay up." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

"I don't care! I'd rather die than hurt Peter!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"He is dying anyway! Why let such a magnificent meal go to waste?
# DO IT.
"-[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

"No!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

You try to throw the book away, but your curse marks flare with blinding, agonizing violet light. Dark, phantom tendrils erupt from the pages, wrapping violently around your arms like iron chains. They forcibly drag your hands toward Peter's motionless chest.

"Once you begin, there is no going back. You belong to me now." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

Your body moves entirely against your will, forced to plunge forward.
**The screen cuts violently to absolute pitch-black.**
In the darkness, a horrific, wet tearing sound echoes—a soul being forcibly ripped apart and absorbed.

*(A long, suffocating moment of silence passes in the blackness...)*

The camera snaps back to the game world.
You stand frozen over a quiet pile of faint, grey ash where Peter once lay.
On your UI, the **Soul Counter** clicks upward.
There is no heroic music—only the roaring crackle of the flames engulfing your home.

"Look at your hands, human. Do you feel it? The warmth? That is the flame of a pure soul. Don't let it go to waste. We have an infestation to finish cleansing." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

You regain control. Your character's posture is now slouched, trembling with shock. There is no magical aura scaring people—just a horrified kid walking through a burning nightmare.
## 👑 The Raid Boss Encounter
You step into the ruined town square. Standing atop the shattered steps of the Town Hall is the massive commander of the attack: The [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Raid Boss\|Raid Boss]]. He turns, brandishing a bloodstained greataxe.

"So, you're the brat who's been killing my scouts?! I'm going to paint these walls with your guts!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Raid Boss\|Raid Boss]]

He doesn't sense dark magic. He doesn't know what you are. He just sees a kid holding a weapon standing in his way.

"An arrogant brute. Cleave him down, partner. I can taste the raw energy simmering inside him from here." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]
## ⚔️ BOSS BATTLE: Raid Boss
The Arena triggers. A high-stakes encounter where you test your parrying against heavy, telegraphed axe swings and utilize your newly unlocked [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Spells/Dark Blast\|Dark Blast]].
The Boss crashes to his knees, defeated, before collapsing face-first into the burning rubble. The Tome instantly reaps his soul.
## 🗺️ The Bitter Departure
You stand amidst the ashes of your home. The flames reflect in your eyes. The horrifying reality of what you did to Peter sits heavily in your stomach.
You turn to leave, but a glint of parchment catches your eye. Tucked into the Raid Boss's heavy armor is a detailed, dust-smudged tactical map.

"Take it. A hunter always maps out his next feeding ground." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

You pull the map free. The screen shifts to a hand-drawn continent map UI. Your burning village is crossed out in red. Several other locations are marked with crude icons. One coastal outpost catches your eye: **The Pirate Docks**.

"The Pirate Docks..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"A lawless hive of cutthroats, murderers, and thieves. Perfect. No one will miss them, and their souls will taste exquisite. Let us set sail, partner." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

Your character turns their back on the flaming ruins of their childhood, embarking on a dark journey.
