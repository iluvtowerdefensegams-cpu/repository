# Chapter 8: A Scientist's Headquarters

You step through the high-tech, sliding metal doors of the compound. The claustrophobic dread of the Dark Forest instantly vanishes, replaced by the clean, humming energy of a massive, state-of-the-art facility.

The camera pulls back, sweeping upwards to show a grand, panoramic bird's-eye view of the entire headquarters—the training grounds, the smoking chimneys of the forge, and the bustling main plaza—before snapping back to your character standing in front of Lyze.

"Nice of you to drop by. Welcome to my Headquarters!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

"This place is... massive." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"Built it myself! Think of this as your personal safe haven. You can come back here to rest and gear up after your adventures. We’ve got training grounds for you to practice new techniques, a forge to upgrade your weaponry, a bakery to supply you with travel rations, and a fully stocked shop. 

Though... now that I think about it, [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Salariem The Shopkeeper\|Salariem The Shopkeeper]] has been missing for a few hours. He's probably out resupplying somewhere. You should go explore and check out the facilities while I prep the main lab!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

## 🏘️ EXPLORING THE HQ: Meet the Locals

You are free to explore the sprawling base. While the main shop is locked up and empty, the rest of the plaza is teeming with life.

### 🍞 Brandon the Baker (The Kitchen)
*Inspired by Cooking Mama*
You walk into a sweet-smelling bakery, only to be immediately clocked in the head with a heavy cast-iron frying pan.

"DON'T JUST STAND THERE DROOLING! Hands washed, apron on! If you want my bread, you're going to earn it. Do NOT make me whack you again!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Brandon The Baker\|Brandon The Baker]]
*   **The Mechanic:** To unlock cooking and purchase food/recipes, you must complete high-stress, rhythmic cooking lessons. Mess up the timing, and Brandon *will* hit you with the pan again. You can also work shifts here to earn quick cash.

### ⚒️ Smithy Steve (The Forge)
*Inspired by Minecraft's Steve*
Standing by a massive anvil is a blocky, incredibly muscular blacksmith staring intensely into a furnace.

"I... am Steve. As a child, I yearned for the mines. The deep, dark blocks called to me. Now, I shape the metal." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Steve The BlackSmith-Smithy Steve\|Steve The BlackSmith-Smithy Steve]]
*   **The Mechanic:** Steve can upgrade your sword and bow using materials you find. He specializes in highly volatile, gunpowder-related gadgets. Be careful—his upgrade sequences have a tendency to end in a loud, smoking **TNT explosion** that leaves him completely unfazed.

### 🤖 Scratch (The Workshop)
*Inspired by the Scratch learning platform*
In a side workshop filled with orange gears and wires, Lyze's first apprentice is frantically programming a humanoid chassis.

"Welcome to the Scratchpad! I build the future! You help me find high-grade machine parts, and I'll let you deploy your very own combat robots!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Scratch\|Scratch]]
*   **The Mechanic:** Completing his side quests unlocks **Robots** (special summons with their own programmable skill trees). 
*   **The Minigame:** He also features a customization terminal where you can design a custom machine... only for it to immediately turn on you in a "Design a machine to thrash your own ass" Deltarune-style combat challenge.

### 🗑️ Scrappy (The Back Alley Dumpster)
*Inspired by a certain `[[BIG SHOT]]`*
Hidden in the dark, trash-strewn alleyway behind Lyze's main lab, a twitchy, fast-talking merchant pops his head out of a dumpster.

"HEY! YOU! YES, YOU, THE `[[Valued Customer]]`! GOT ANY OF THAT SWEET, SWEET `[5CЯ4P]`? BRING IT TO ME! I GIVE YOU THE MOST `<R4D>` DEALS OF YOUR LIFE! DO NOT TRUST THE `[[Upper Class]]` IN THE LAB! BUY FROM THE TRASH!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Scrappy\|Scrappy]]
*   **The Mechanic:** Bring Scrappy unique scrap metal found on your journeys to trade for highly powerful, chaotic, and sketchy items that you can't get anywhere else. (Btw he sells 1 [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Others/PageStone\|PageStone]])

# The Lab & The Grand Map

You step through the heavy, reinforced blast doors at the back of the plaza. The interior of the main laboratory is a chaotic masterpiece of wires, glowing holographic displays, and mechanical arms. Lyze stands at a central terminal, typing furiously before turning to face you.

"Come in, come in! Welcome, test subject. I have some massive upgrades to discuss with you. Step over to the scanner... Perfect! Let's see what your gear is actually made of." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]] 

She plugs a heavy, glowing cable directly into your Watch. Holographic screens burst into life around you, analyzing your movements.

"Downloading the combat logs now... and, wow. These are incredible results. Here is how your weapon data breaks down: your gear classifies itself into four categories—Free-hand, Melee, Ranged, and Arcane. The more you use a specific style in battle, the more your combat proficiency level increases, unlocking brand-new, lethal abilities! 

But this is just a baseline. I need you out there gathering more real-world combat data. I also wanted to ask you about—wait. What on earth is *that*?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

Lyze's eyes lock onto the ancient, leather-bound book resting at your hip. She reaches out to touch its weathered binding.

*Violently snapping its pages shut, nearly biting her fingers*
"Hands off the merchandise, girl! I am not a museum piece!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

Gasps, stepping back in absolute shock
"Oh... wow. Okay, sorry there, little guy. It... actually talks. Fascinating!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

"Rude. Utterly classless. My partner and I do not have time for your childish curiosity. We are here for power." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

"Power? Well, if it's raw power you're after... do you two actually know what a Chaos Beacon is?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

"No." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*A deep, menacing hum vibrates from its pages*
"I do. They are massive crystalline monoliths containing primordial energy. Whoever shatters their seals and claims that energy will wield power beyond mortal comprehension. And if I am not mistaken, scientist... there is a massive [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Extras/Central Beacon\|Central Beacon]] sitting right in the heart of the capital." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

*Her expression instantly hardens, becoming serious*
"The [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Extras/Central Beacon\|Central Beacon]]? Are you insane? That is the strongest beacon in existence, and it is heavily guarded by the Prince himself. If you try to touch that right now, he will vaporize you. I strongly suggest you locate and siphon the other four regional Beacons first if you want to stand a chance." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

"We will butcher the Prince without a second thought. But... to ensure absolute victory, we must first assemble the rest of the **[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Extras/Dark Set\|Dark Set]]**." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

"The [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Extras/Dark Set\|Dark Set]]? What's that?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

"When my original creator forged me, he also crafted a legendary sword and a suit of armor to complete the set. When the three pieces are reunited, the wielder becomes an unstoppable force of nature. We must find them." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

> **⚠️ [LORE NOTE: The Tome's Deception]**
> *The Tome is lying. The 'Dark Set' are not armor pieces—they are the physical fragments of **Skotos**, the primordial entity of darkness, who was defeated and sealed away. The Tome is using your cursed body as a puppet to gather his fragmented soul. If you bring the Dark Set to the Central Beacon with enough harvested souls, Skotos will be reborn, and you will be erased.*

*Skeptical, but typing on her console* "Right... well, a magic sword and armor sound useful, I guess. I've patched the locations of the regional [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Extras/Chaos Beacons\|Chaos Beacons]] directly to your Watch. Good luck." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

Your watch pings. A massive overlay of the world map displays four shining beacons and the looming threat of the capital in the center.

*Its pages tremble, sensing something in the air*
"I... I can feel it. The Dark Sword is near. It's calling to me from a high place... a place of cold stone." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]


"High place? That's got to be [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Locations/Wignus Tower\|Wignus Tower]], just past the valley." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

"[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Wignus The Wizard\|Wignus]]... That miserable, wretched insect." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

"You know him?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"It was *he* who cursed my pages with this insatiable hunger for souls! We owe him a very personal, very bloody visit. Let us tear his tower to the ground!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

> **⚠️ [LORE NOTE: The Curse]**
> *Wignus was a loyal follower of the light primordial, Lampros. He didn't curse the Tome out of malice; he sealed the dark entity inside it, forcing the set to crave souls merely to sustain and prevent the dark essence from leaking out.*

"Well, if you're heading toward the tower, your path runs right through the old theme park. It's got the first [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Extras/Chaos Beacons\|Chaos Beacon]] inside. By the way, [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Salariem The Shopkeeper\|Salariem The Shopkeeper]] just got back to his shop. He actually knows the park's owner—you should go talk to him before you leave." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

## 🎪 Talking to Salariem the Shopkeeper

You walk back out to the plaza. Salariem's shop is wide open, decorated with colorful banners. The moment you step close, Salariem begins playing a wild, incredibly catchy tune on his accordion, dancing on his heels.

🎶 *"Step right up, step right up! Heavy swords or shiny rings, Salariem sells the finest things! Welcome, my friend! What can I do for you on this glorious day?"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Salariem The Shopkeeper\|Salariem The Shopkeeper]] (subject to change)

"What do you know about the theme park on the way to Wignus Tower?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*Instantly stops playing, gasping dramatically, and begins talking at a blistering, hyper-fast speed, gesticulating wildly with his hands*
"Oh! You mean **[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Locations/Super Fun Land\|Super Fun Land]]**! Where the fun *never* ends! Or, well, where the fun *used* to never end! It's run by my dear, wonderful, absolutely cuckoo-in-the-brain old friend, **Septer**!  

Though, sad story, really—nobody has set foot in that park for years. Septer went completely off his rocker! He filled the entire place with automated, killer steam-robots, and he's running the whole park using a glowing Chaos Beacon as a battery! 

You're going there to take it, aren't you? Oh, I knew it! If you fight him, do me a favor and try not to break his Sceptre—I absolutely *adore* his Sceptre. Might even buy it off you if you bring it back!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Salariem The Shopkeeper\|Salariem The Shopkeeper]]

## 🛣️ The Road to Super Fun Land

You leave the safety of the HQ, following the map coordinates. The sky grows dim as you trek down the mountain path.

*   **⚔️ The Bandit Encounter:** Along the rocky road, you run into a group of four hostile bandits. They are coughing and searching the cliffs, muttering about finding a "poisoner's hideout" to settle a deadly score. 
    *   Due to a thick, green toxic fog blocking the way to the hideout, they cannot proceed. Frustrated and desperate, they spot you and draw their weapons.
    *   *The Combat:* You easily dispatch the four bandits, harvesting their strength.
*   **🏔️ The Trial Cave:** Nearby, you spot a glowing cave entrance—another one of **Lyze's Trials**. It is guarded by high-level monsters. Beating the trial doesn't yield souls (as they are artificial constructs), but completing it rewards you with valuable upgrade materials.

Wiping the dust from your blade, you stand before the rusted, towering iron gates of *[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Locations/Super Fun Land\|Super Fun Land]]*. The creepy, distorted sound of carnival music echoes from within...
