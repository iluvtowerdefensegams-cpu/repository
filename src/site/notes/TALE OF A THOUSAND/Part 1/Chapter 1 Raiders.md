## 🤝 Peter Joins the Chores
You step out of your house and into the peaceful, sunlit plaza of your [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Locations/Town\|Town]]. Before you can even take ten steps, a familiar voice calls out as a hand slaps you on the shoulder.

"Yo, `<username>`! Going to the Market? Perfect, I'll walk with you. I actually wanted to show you something anyway!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

"Hey, Peter. Yeah, Dad has me running some errands." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"Awesome, let's get to it. Man, your dad's knees must really be killing him with this damp air. Let's grab that satchel first!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

Peter tags along with you. As you walk through the town, he comments on the NPCs, complains about how overpriced the market items are, and generally acts as your loyal, joking best friend. You complete the chores together.

"Alright, that's the medicine secured! Hey, I'll head over to the [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Locations/Fountain\|Fountain]] by the [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Locations/Library\|Library]] and wait for you there while you drop that off to your dad. Don't take all day, sleepyhead!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

You return to the kitchen and hand over the medicine to your father.

"Ah, thank you, `<username>`. You're a lifesaver. Go on and enjoy your day now."

"Where is it... I swear I saw it here yesterday. Help me find a book called 'Arcane Curses,' would you?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]
## 🔍 Quest: Find the Book
You search the library. Once you interact with the correct glowing shelf and pull the book down, you hand it to Peter, who quickly flips to the page he bookmarked.

"Here it is! Listen to this: *'This [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Others/Curse\|Curse]] is incredibly rare. Only three cases have been documented in all of history. The host presenting this curse will develop strange, glowing rune marks across their arms. If the marks light up, the host will die within the next three minutes...'* Wow. I knew it was bad, but not *that* bad." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

"That's... terrifying." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"Wait, it doesn't end there. Look at this next paragraph. It says you can survive even after they light up. It says: *'A sacrifice must be made.'* ...What does that even mean?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

Peter looks up from the book, closing it slowly with a soft sigh.

"A sacrifice? Man, magic books are so dramatic. I can't imagine ever hurting someone else just to save my own skin. Could you?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

"I... I don't know." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"Well, I know *I* couldn't. Honestly? If it ever came down to it... I'd rather go out making sure you're safe. That's what friends do." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]
# 💥 BOOM!
A deafening explosion rattles the entire library, blowing out the stained-glass windows. Dust and debris rain down from the wooden ceiling.

"What on earth was that?!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]

You both rush out of the library. The peaceful town from this morning is completely gone—swallowed by roaring fire, black smoke, and screaming citizens.

"RAIDERS!!! THE RAIDERS ARE HERE!" -Randy Number 1

"There's too many of them! Run to the [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Locations/Forest\|Forest]]! I'll draw them away and catch up to you! Go!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]
## 🏃 The Escape: Running Blind
You sprint blindly into the tree line of the dark forest. But as you cross the threshold of the trees, a cold, echoing voice—**The Nightmare**, the exact titanic voice from your dream—booms directly inside your mind, shaking your very soul.
# "DIE."
Your [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Others/Curse\|marks]] instantly light up with a violent, agonizing violet glow. A countdown timer appears at the top of the screen. Your vision blurs, and your character stumbles heavily, occasionally collapsing to the dirt in sheer physical weakness.
Behind you, snapping jaws and glowing red eyes emerge from the smoke—the Nightmare's beasts are hunting you. With no weapons, you must run.
 * **Gameplay Mechanic:** Manage your stamina and stumbling animations while navigating the chaotic, root-filled forest path. Faint violet embers on the ground guide you toward a hidden cave.
With the timer dangerously close to zero, you scramble into the mouth of a dark cave. The beasts screech and halt at the entrance, seemingly terrified of whatever lies inside.
## 📖 The Dark Pact
"Come."
Driven by the whisper, you stumble deeper into the quiet cavern. On a stone pedestal, a leather-bound book levitates in the air, radiating a heavy, suffocating dark light.

"Tick-tock, human. Thirty seconds left. Your blood is turning to ice, isn't it?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

"W-what...?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"I am [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]. Those mindless beasts outside didn't stop because they pitied you—they stopped because they fear *Me*. And right now, I am the only thing standing between you and an agonizing death." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

You look down at your arms. The curse marks are burning, practically blinding in the dark.

"A pathetic wizard locked my true power away, leaving me bound to this pitiful soul-reaping state. But I still have enough trickery left to freeze that little curse of yours in place. The question is... what are you going to give me for it?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

"Me? What do you want from me?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"Your absolute compliance. You will feed me souls. A hundred of them should suffice to break my bindings, along with my missing pages scattered across this wretched land. Do we have a deal, or would you rather see what happens when that timer hits zero?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]
## 🗳️ The Choice: Life or Ashes
 * **Option A: Accept**
   * *Standard/Easy:* "A wise choice. Let us begin."
   * *Hard Mode:* "Good choice, Partner. Or should I say... *My Special One?*"
 * **Option B: Refuse**
   * *Easy/Normal Difficulties:* The Tome laughs as your screen shakes and the timer hits 3 seconds. *"Oh, please. You don't have the luxury of a spine right now. Try again."* (Forces the prompt back up).
   * *Hard Mode:* The Tome closes itself with a heavy thud. *"Suit yourself. I'll find someone else."* (The timer hits zero. You instantly disintegrate. **GAME OVER**).
## 🔗 The Pact Signed
Upon accepting, the dark violet light from the book surges into your arm, forcibly anchoring the curse. The timer vanishes, replaced by a permanent Soul Counter UI element.

"Excellent. Now pick me up. Your precious little village is burning to the ground as we speak, and I wouldn't want you to miss the buffet." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

You grab the Tome and rush back toward the smoke of your village.
