# "DIE"
Your [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Others/Curse\|marks]] instantly light up with a violent glow. A countdown timer appears on the screen, scaling based on your difficulty level. Your vision blurs, and your character stumbles heavily, occasionally collapsing to the ground from weakness.
Behind you, snapping jaws and glowing eyes emerge from the smoke—hungry beasts are hunting you down. With no weapons to fight back, you have no choice but to run.
## 🏃‍♂️ The Escape: Running Blind

*   **Gameplay Mechanic:** The player must navigate the chaotic forest path while managing their stumbling animation.
*   **The Halfway Mark:** When the timer hits the halfway point, a dark, mysterious voice begins whispering, guiding you toward a hidden cave *(On easier difficulties, faint dark energy arrows appear on the ground to show the way)*

With the timer dangerously close to zero, you scramble into the mouth of the cave. The beasts screech and halt at the entrance, seemingly terrified of whatever lies inside.
# Come.
Driven by the whisper, you stumble deeper into the cavern. You find a book levitating above a stone pedestal, radiating a heavy, suffocating dark light.
Frightened, yet desperate, you step closer.
"Tick-tock, human. Thirty seconds left. Your blood is turning to ice, isn't it?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]
"W-what...?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]
"I am The Dark Tome. Those mindless beasts outside didn't stop because they pitied you—they stopped because they fear *Me*. And right now, I am the only thing standing between you and an agonizing death." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]
You look down at your arms. The curse marks are burning, practically blinding in the dark.
"A pathetic wizard locked my true power away, leaving me bound to this pitiful soul-reaping state. But I still have enough trickery left to freeze that little curse of yours in place. The question is... what are you going to give me for it?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]
"Me? What do you want from me?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]
"Your absolute compliance. You will feed me souls. A hundred of them should suffice to break my bindings, along with my missing pages scattered across this wretched land. Do we have a deal, or would you rather see what happens when that timer hits zero?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

### 🗳️ The Choice: Life or Ashes

*   **Option A: Accept**
    *   *Standard/Easy:* "A wise choice. Let us begin."
    *   *Hard Mode (If you won the intro fight):* "Good choice, Partner. Or should I say... *My Special One?*"

*   **Option B: Refuse**
    *   *Easy/Normal Difficulties:* The Tome laughs as your screen shakes and the timer hits 3 seconds. *"Oh, please. You don't have the luxury of a spine right now. Try again."* (Forces the prompt back up).
    *   *Hard Mode:* The Tome closes itself with a heavy thud. *"Suit yourself. I'll find someone else."* (The timer hits zero. You instantly disintegrate. **GAME OVER**).
### The Pact Signed

Upon accepting, the dark light from the book surges into your arm, forcibly anchoring the curse. The timer vanishes, replaced by a permanent Soul Counter UI element.
"Excellent. Now pick me up. Your precious little village is burning to the ground as we speak, and I wouldn't want you to miss the buffet." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]
You pick up the Tome and rush back to your village.
