# Chapter 15: Back Into The Dark Forest
The freezing mist of the mountain ridge fades back into the dense, suffocating canopy of the Dark Forest shortcut. But just as you reach the iron-wrought gates marking the boundary, the shadows ahead twist. Two figures step out into the clearing, shoulder to shoulder.
To the left stands Samuel, his spectacles cracked, holding a fluttering, burnt page from *The Tale* in his trembling hand. To the right stands Jack, idly tossing one of his massive battle axes from hand to hand, standing slightly in front of his brother like an unyielding wall. Their expressions are dead serious.

"I told you I'd see you again, kid," Jack grunts, his casual grin completely gone as he glances back at his panicking brother. "My bro here says you're a walking nightmare... that you're going to tear this whole world apart. I don't care about all that lore nonsense, but nobody touches Samuel while I'm breathing!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Jack The Two-Axed\|Jack The Two-Axed]]

"The text... the page predicted your return!" 
-Samuel stammers, pushing his cracked glasses up his nose, trying desperately to sound arrogant despite his terror. "I—I was wrong about the story! You aren't a champion... you're the calamity! But Jack and I... we are the final exam you won't pass!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Samuel The Storymaster\|Samuel The Storymaster]]
## ⚔️ BOSS BATTLE: The Puzzling Brothers
### 🧩 MECHANICS: The Quiz-Obby Gauntlet
This fight shatters standard combat. The arena becomes a shifting, dynamic nightmare where you must balance rapid platforming with lore comprehension under fire.
 * **The Mix-Up:** Jack slams his axes into the ground, violently warping the terrain into a floating, unstable obby, placing himself directly between you and Samuel. The paths split into three distinct routes (A, B, and C), each leading to a different elevated platform.
 * **The Volley:** Samuel hovers in the back, firing magical projectile runes while shouting out rapid-fire questions about the world's lore.
 * **The Execution:** You must use your **Grappling Hook** to swing through the obby, dodging Jack's incoming projectile axes, and land explicitly on the platform that represents the *correct* answer to disable Samuel's warding shield.
 * **High Risk/Reward:** Land on the right platform, and Samuel suffers massive recoil damage while Jack gets briefly stunned. Land on the wrong platform—or fall into the void—and Samuel casts a high-tier spell that restores their shared health bar while blasting your HP.
With a final, perfectly timed grapple, you strike the ultimate correct platform. The magical feedback triggers a violent explosion. The floating obby shatters, and both brothers crash heavily onto the broken stone steps of the shortcut gate.
Jack falls over Samuel, shielding his younger brother with his own body until the very end. Their forms begin to flicker and turn to dark ash.

"Jack... I'm sorry... I wrote us... into a corner..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Samuel The Storymaster\|Samuel The Storymaster]]

"Ah... don't sweat it, bro... we gave 'em... a good fight..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Jack The Two-Axed\|Jack The Two-Axed]]

They dissolve completely, leaving behind two glowing purple souls that fly violently into your dark blade.
### ⚙️ ARRIVAL: Lyze HQ
You push open the pneumatic doors of the high-tech sanctuary. The machines hum, and cooling fans whir. **Salariem**, **Steve the Blacksmith**, and **Scrappy** are set up at their usual stations in the central hub.
Lyze's holographic avatar blinks into existence on the main console, her digital eyes narrowing as she scans your biometric readouts.

*"You made it back down. Good. But your vitals... your body temperature is dangerously high, and your heart rate is completely off the charts. What happened out there? And why is that creature carrying Septer's wand while a creepy doll floats over your shoulder?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

​Puppy tilts her head with a synthetic, silent giggle while Scepter pulses with faint residual Funergy. You ignore the question entirely, stepping right up to the terminal.

*"I need the antenna piece. And the thermal suit."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*"Right, the hardware,"* [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]] says, playing dumb while surreptitiously logging your dark energy signatures to her secure drives. *"Salariem has the antenna synchronization part locked in the secure vault, but he's not giving it up for free. Meanwhile... we've been monitoring regional evacuation sectors. We managed to pull a small group of refugees out of your old home village before the total blackout hit. They're safely cataloged in our lower shelters."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

The screen flashes with a scrolling text log of survivors.

*The pages rustle with a low, sinister purr at your hip.* "Refugees? How thoughtful of them to gather in one convenient place, partner. Keep that layout memorized. We'll clean them up later." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

You scan the scrolling list of names, your eyes stopping cold near the bottom of the log.

*"...He isn't on here."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*"Who?"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

*"My father. When the village fell... I found Peter's body lying in the ash. But my father's... it wasn't there. I checked everywhere."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*"We didn't find any sign of him either. If he isn't in the casualty reports and he didn't make it to our evac trucks... he's just gone. I'm sorry."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

Your character stares at the screen, a brief, heavy silence filling the lab before the dark runes on your arm pulse violently, forcing your focus back to the blade. He's alive. Somewhere out there.
### 🌿 Salariem’s Bargain
Salariem slides over the counter, strumming a loud, comedic chord on his lute with a wide, theatrical grin.

*"Aha! A somber mood requires a joyful tune! Welcome back, dark and broody traveler! I hear you want my shiny little antenna piece, eh? Well, I don't give away top-tier tech for free! I need a little favor for a... let's call them a 'close friend' of mine."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Salariem The Shopkeeper\|Salariem The Shopkeeper]]

*"What do you want?"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*"Deep within the untouched layers of the Dark Forest grows a highly volatile, legendary blossom—the Orchid of Power. The local wildlife won't go near it, but its raw energy is exactly what I need. Go fetch it, bring it back, and the antenna part is all yours! Oh, but watch out... a rather protective lady guards that grove, and she doesn't take kindly to weeds like you!""* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Salariem The Shopkeeper\|Salariem The Shopkeeper]]
