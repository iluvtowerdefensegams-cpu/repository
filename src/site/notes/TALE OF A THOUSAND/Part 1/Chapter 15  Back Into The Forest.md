# Chapter 15: Back Into The Dark Forest
The freezing mist of the mountain ridge fades into the dense, suffocating canopy of the Dark Forest shortcut. But just as you reach the iron-wrought gates marking the boundary, the shadows ahead twist. Two figures step out into the clearing, shoulder to shoulder.
To the left stands Samuel, his spectacles cracked, nursing a massive bruise from your earlier encounter. To the right stands Jack, idly tossing one of his massive battle axes from hand to hand. Their expressions are grim. There is no running away this time.

"I told you the road only gets steeper, kid. But this is the end of the line for us. We're putting everything we've got left into this." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Jack The Two-Axed\|Jack The Two-Axed]]

"He who laughs last laughs best! This is our masterpiece, bearer of the Tome. Together, we are the Puzzling Brothers—and this is our absolute final exam!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Samuel The Storymaster\|Samuel The Storymaster]]
### ⚔️ BOSS BATTLE: The Puzzling Brothers (The Final Puzzle)
### 🧩 MECHANICS: The Quiz-Obby Gauntlet
This fight shatters standard combat. The arena is a shifting, dynamic nightmare where you must balance rapid platforming with lore comprehension.
 * **The Mix-Up:** Jack slams his axes into the ground, violently warping the terrain into a floating, unstable obby. The paths instantly split into three distinct routes (A, B, and C), each leading to a different platform.
 * **The Volley:** Samuel hovers above, firing magical projectile runes while shouting out high-speed lore trivia.
 * **The Execution:** You must use your grappling hook to swing through the obby, dodging Jack's incoming projectile axes, and land explicitly on the platform that represents the *correct* answer.
 * **High Risk/Reward:** Land on the right platform, and Samuel suffers massive damage while Jack gets briefly stunned. Land on a wrong platform—or fall into the void—and Samuel casts a high-tier spell that fully restores a portion of their shared health bar while blasting your HP.
With a final, perfectly timed grapple, you strike the ultimate correct platform. The magical feedback triggers a massive, violent explosion. The floating obby shatters into dust, and both brothers crash heavily onto the broken stone steps of the shortcut gate.
Their forms begin to flicker and turn to ash.

"A... perfect score... A beautiful... final act..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Samuel The Storymaster\|Samuel The Storymaster]]

"Heh... you really... broke the puzzle, kid... Good luck... on the peak..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Jack The Two-Axed\|Jack The Two-Axed]]

They dissolve completely, leaving nothing behind but two glowing purple souls that violently fly into your blade.
### ⚙️ ARRIVAL: Lyze HQ
You push open the pneumatic doors of the high-tech sanctuary. The monitors hum, and cooling fans whir. **Salariem**, **Steve the Blacksmith**, and **Scrappy** are all set up at their usual stations, their services already fully active from your previous visits.

Lyze's holographic avatar blinks into existence on the central console, her digital eyes immediately narrowing as she scans your appearance.

*"You made it back down. Good. But your vitals... they're completely off the charts. What happened out there? And what is that creepy doll floating over your shoulder?"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

Puppy floatingly tilts her head, letting out a tiny, synthetic giggle. You ignore the question, stepping closer to the terminal.

*"I need the antenna piece. And the cold suit."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*"Right, the hardware. Salariem has the antenna synchronization part locked in the secure vault, but it's not going anywhere until you help him out. But more importantly... we've been monitoring the evacuation sectors. We managed to pull a few small groups of refugees out of your old home village before the total blackout hit. They're safely cataloged in our lower shelters."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

The screen flashes with a scrolling text log of survivors.

*The pages rustle with a low, sinister purr.*
"Refugees? How thoughtful of them to gather in one convenient place, partner. Keep that layout memorized. We'll clean them up later." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

You scan the scrolling list of names, your eyes stopping cold at the bottom of the log.

*"...He isn't on here."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*"Who?"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

*"My father. When the village fell... I found Peter's body lying in the ash. But my father's... it wasn't there. I checked everywhere."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*"We didn't find any sign of him either. If he isn't in the casualty reports and he didn't make it to our evac trucks... he's just gone. I'm sorry."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]
 
Your character stares at the screen, a brief, heavy silence filling the lab before the dark runes on your arm pulse violently, forcing your focus back to the blade. He's alive. Somewhere out there.
### 🌿 Salariem’s Bargain
Salariem slides over the counter, strumming a loud, comedic chord on his lute with a wide, theatrical grin.

*"Aha! A somber mood requires a joyful tune! Welcome back, dark and broody traveler! I hear you want my shiny little antenna piece, eh? Well, I don't give away top-tier tech for free! I need a little favor for a... let's call them a 'close friend' of mine."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Salariem The Shopkeeper\|Salariem The Shopkeeper]]

*"What do you want?"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*"Deep within the untouched layers of the Dark Forest grows a highly volatile, deep crimson herb. The local wildlife hates it, but it's exactly what I need. Go fetch it, bring it back, and the antenna part is all yours! Oh, but watch out... a rather protective lady guards that grove, and she doesn't take kindly to weeds like you!"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Salariem The Shopkeeper\|Salariem The Shopkeeper]]
