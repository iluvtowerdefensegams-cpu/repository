# Chapter 14: The Steep Ascent
You step out of the damp, collapsing cavern into the blinding light of the northern mountain ridge. The air is noticeably thinner, and the rocky trail ahead winds steeply upward toward the frost-line. But before you can even take a breath of fresh air, a heavy, metallic *thud* shakes the stone path beneath your boots.
Standing on a narrow ridge blocking the path is a massive, broad-shouldered warrior. He casually rests two colossal, notched battle axes over his shoulders, grinning wildly through a rugged beard.
"So you're the one who just wrecked Poppy's theater? She always was too dramatic for her own good. I prefer a much more... hands-on approach. I'm Jack the Two-Axed, and your little show ends right here!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Jack The Two-Axed\|Jack The Two-Axed]]
### 🪓 BOSS BATTLE: Jack The Two-Axed
### ⚔️ PHASE 1: The Heavy Executioner
 * **The Combat:** Jack is a hyper-aggressive powerhouse. He leaps across the arena, delivering massive ground-slams with his twin axes that create shockwaves you have to jump over.
 * **The Strategy:** You have to time your dashes perfectly to get behind his heavy recovery animations, using **Puppy** to distract him with reanimated puppet decoys while you slash at his blind spots.
With a heavy strike, you crack his guard. Jack stumbles back, laughing hoarsely instead of flinching. He slams the hilts of his twin axes into the mountain stone.
"Not bad, kid! But let's see how you handle the high ground! Up you go!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Jack The Two-Axed\|Jack The Two-Axed]]
### 🧗 PHASE 2: The Vertical Grappling Obby
 * **The Shift:** The impact of his axes triggers a localized earthquake, causing massive stone pillars and platforms to erupt out of the cliffside, floating precariously over a deadly drop. Jack leaps to the very top platform, out of reach of your standard attacks.
 * **The Execution:** You must instantly whip out your **Grappling Hook**. The gameplay transforms into a high-speed, vertical obstacle course (obby). You have to swing across collapsing silhouette crags, dodge giant swinging logs, and grapple upwards under a strict time limit.
 * **The Twist:** While you are climbing, Jack stands at the top throwing a barrage of projectile axes down at you. If an axe hits you mid-swing, it knocks you back down to the previous checkpoint platform.
You execute a flawless final grapple, flying through the air and landing a crushing blow right across Jack's chest. He crashes into the dirt, coughing up a laugh as the floating platforms collapse into the valley below.

"Heh... you really... pack a punch, don't ya? Go on then... the road only gets steeper from here." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Jack The Two-Axed\|Jack The Two-Axed]]

Jack rolls over, completely exhausted but alive. Your soul counter doesn't move.
### ⚖️ THE JUDGEMENT: A Cold Warning
Leaving Jack behind, you push forward onto the true mountain border. The vibrant colors of the lower world begin to fade, replaced by pale grey stones and a light, freezing mist.
The path suddenly levels out into a long, eerie stone bridge stretching across a massive chasm. Standing dead center in the middle of the bridge, completely alone, is **Mike**.
There is no music. No combat UI appears. No health bars fade into view.
Mike doesn't draw a weapon. He just stands there, his hands shoved into his pockets, staring at you with a cold, unblinking intensity that completely mirrors Sans' iconic judgment stance.

"Stop. Right there." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]

*"...Mike."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*"Look at you. Look at that sword. Look at the runes on your arm. I've been watching you since the docks, hoping—praying—that maybe you were just trying to survive. But you've crossed a line. You tore through Super Fun Land. You brought down Wignus' tower. And now you've silenced Poppy."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]

The wind howls across the chasm, rattling the pages of the Dark Tome at your hip.

*"I'm not here to fight you today. Not yet. You think you're getting stronger, but you're just becoming a monster. This is your final warning. Turn back. If you take one more step down this dark path... I won't hold back. And trust me... everything you've faced so far? That was the easy part. From this point onward... I will make your life a brutal hell."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]
 
Mike steps back into the freezing mist, vanishing from the bridge as quickly as he appeared.
The screen flashes with a stark, ominous text overlay:
> **[WARNING: From this point onward, the world has grown hostile. Enemies will attack with absolute malice. The game has become brutally harder.]**

### 🏔️ QUEST START: The Mountain Antenna
You cross the bridge, the temperature plummeting instantly. Your character begins to shiver violently, their movement speed slowing down to a crawl as ice crystals form on the edges of the screen.
Suddenly, a static-filled holographic projection blinks into existence from your pocket gadget. It's **Lyze**.

*"Hey, test subject! Do you copy? I'm tracking your position near the northern peak. There's a massive communication antenna up there, but it's missing the final synchronization part. If you can climb up there and place the piece, I can calibrate the satellite grid and unlock full Teleportation (TP) capabilities across the entire world map! ...Wait, looking at my thermal readings, your body temperature is dropping dangerously low. Do NOT try to scale the peak yet! You don't have cold resistance. You'll freeze to death before you reach the top!"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

*"How do I get up there then?"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*"There's a hidden, narrow border shortcut that cuts right back down into the Dark Forest. Use it to sneak back to my HQ. Salariem has the duplicate antenna part and a protective thermal suit in the inventory labs. Get down there, grab the gear, and get back up here before the frost sets in!"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

The hologram cuts out. With the mountain trail blocked by the freezing cold, you turn toward the narrow, shadowed path leading down into the dense canopies of the Dark Forest hub.
