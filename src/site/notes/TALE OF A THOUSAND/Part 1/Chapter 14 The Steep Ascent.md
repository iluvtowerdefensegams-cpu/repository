# Chapter 14: The Steep Ascent
You step out of the damp, collapsing cavern into the blinding light of the northern mountain ridge. The air is noticeably thinner, and the rocky trail ahead winds steeply upward toward the frost-line.
Standing on a narrow ridge blocking the path, stretching his arms, is a massive, broad-shouldered warrior. He casually rests two colossal, notched battle axes over his shoulders, grinning like a massive goofball through a rugged beard. He turns around, sees you, and gives a cheerful, oblivious wave.

"Hey bro! Didn't expect anyone else to be taking the high road today!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Jack The Two-Axed\|Jack The Two-Axed]]

You don't say a word. You slowly draw the Dark Sword, stepping forward with zero hesitation. Seeing him look up at the mountain clouds, distracted, you lunge forward in a ruthless, silent execution strike aimed directly at his exposed spine—
# *SWOOSH!*
Without even turning around, Jack’s raw survival instincts trigger. In a blink of an eye, his massive body blurs and he takes a colossal, effortless leap twenty feet backward, landing with terrifying agility on a narrow stone ledge. His cheerful grin morphs into a wide, wild battle laugh as he realizes what you just tried to do.

"Whoa there! Going straight for the backstab? I like your style, kid! But if it's a fight you want, let's see how long you can last against the brawn!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Jack The Two-Axed\|Jack The Two-Axed]]
### 🪓 BOSS BATTLE: Jack The Two-Axed
### ⚔️ PHASE 1: The Heavy Executioner
 * **The Combat:** Jack is an absolute powerhouse—dumb on strategy, but terrifyingly fast. He leaps across the arena, delivering massive ground-slams with his twin axes that create shockwaves you have to jump over.
 * **The Strategy:** You have to time your dashes perfectly to get behind his heavy recovery animations, using the **Puppy** summon to distract his attention while you slash at his flanks.
With a heavy strike, you crack his guard. Jack stumbles back, laughing hoarsely instead of flinching. He slams the hilts of his twin axes into the mountain stone.
"Not bad! But let's take this to the high ground! Catch me if you can!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Jack The Two-Axed\|Jack The Two-Axed]]
### 🧗 PHASE 2: The Vertical Grappling Obby
 * **The Shift:** The impact of his axes triggers a localized earthquake, causing massive stone pillars and platforms to erupt out of the cliffside, floating precariously over a deadly drop. Jack leaps straight to the very top platform with effortless agility.
 * **The Execution:** You whip out your **Grappling Hook**. The gameplay transforms into a high-speed vertical obstacle course. You must swing across collapsing crags, dodge giant swinging logs, and grapple upwards under a strict time limit.
 * **The Twist:** Jack treats the entire thing like a game, throwing a barrage of heavy axes down at you with terrifying precision. Getting hit mid-swing knocks you back down to the previous checkpoint platform.
You execute a flawless final grapple, flying through the air and landing a crushing slash across Jack's chest plate. He crashes onto the ledge, coughing up a loud laugh as the floating platforms collapse into the valley below.

"Heh... man, you really... pack a punch, don't ya? Alright, alright... you win this round!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Jack The Two-Axed\|Jack The Two-Axed]]

You step forward, raising the Dark Sword with a cold, merciless glare to claim his soul. The Tome at your hip flips open, its pages fluttering in hungry anticipation.
*The Escape:* Before your blade can descend, Jack slams his palms into the stone floor. With a colossal **BOOM**, his insane legs propel him straight into the clouds with a massive super-jump, vanishing over the mountain peak
!
*(Its pages snapping shut in pure rage)* "COWARD! He leaped straight out of our reach! Ugh, no matter... we will harvest him later." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

(A low, disappointed hum vibrating through the obsidian hilt) "Master... the meat escaped... but the scent of blood remains on the trail..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Equipment/Weapons/The Dark Sword\|The Dark Sword]]
### ⚖️ THE JUDGEMENT: A Cold Warning
Leaving Jack behind, you push forward onto the true mountain border. The vibrant colors of the lower world begin to fade, replaced by pale grey stones and a light, freezing mist.
The path levels out into a long, eerie stone bridge stretching across a massive chasm. Standing dead center in the middle of the bridge, completely alone, is **Mike**.
There is no music. No combat UI appears. No health bars fade into view.
Mike doesn't draw a weapon. He just stands there, hands in his pockets, staring at you with a cold, unblinking intensity.

"Stop right there." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]

*"...Mike."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*"Look at you. Look at that sword. Look at the dark runes creeping up your arm. I've been watching you since the docks, keeping track of your path. I hoped you were just trying to survive out here... but you're enjoying this, aren't you?"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]

The wind howls across the chasm, rattling the pages of the Dark Tome at your hip.

*"I'm not here to fight you today. Not yet. You think you're getting stronger, but you're just becoming a monster. This is your warning. Turn back. If you take one more step down this path... I won't hold back next time. And trust me... everything you've faced so far? That was the easy part."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]

Mike steps back into the freezing mist, vanishing from the bridge into thin air.
The screen flashes with a stark, ominous text overlay:
> **[WARNING: The world has grown hostile. Enemies will attack with absolute malice. The game has become brutally harder.]**
### 🏔️ QUEST START: The Mountain Antenna
You cross the bridge, the temperature plummeting instantly. Your character begins to shiver, movement speed dropping to a crawl as frost forms on the edges of the screen.
Suddenly, a static-filled holographic projection blinks into existence from your pocket gadget. It's **Lyze**
.
*"Hey, test subject! Do you copy? I'm tracking your position near the northern peak. There's a massive communication antenna up there, but it's missing the final synchronization part. If you place it, I can calibrate the satellite grid and unlock full Teleportation (TP) capabilities across the map!"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

*(She pauses, squinting at her monitor displays on her end, acting entirely clueless about your dark path while secretly logging your biometric spikes)*
*"...Huh. Why did your heart rate just spike? Anyway, looking at my thermal readings, your body temperature is dropping dangerously low. Do NOT try to scale the peak yet! You don't have cold resistance. You'll freeze to death before you reach the top!"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

*"How do I get up there then?"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*"There's a narrow border shortcut that cuts right back down into the Dark Forest. Use it to sneak back to my HQ. Salariem has the duplicate antenna part and a protective thermal suit. Get down there, grab the gear, and get back up here before the frost sets in!"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

The hologram cuts out. With the main mountain trail blocked by the freezing cold, you turn toward the narrow, shadowed path leading down into the dense canopies of the Dark Forest hub.
