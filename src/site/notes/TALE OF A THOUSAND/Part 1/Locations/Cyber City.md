### 🛍️ District 1: The Outskirts & Cyber Markets
 * **The Vibe:** Drenched in flickering neon signs, holographic advertisements, and crowded black-market stalls built out of rusted shipping containers.
 * **Key Features:**
   * **The Augment Market:** Trade leftover scrap for tech utility upgrades (e.g., thermal scope attachments, EMP charges).
   * **The Gates:** The upper city is sealed off by heavy security laser grids. To get inside, you either need to bribe security or get a clearance keycard from the underground gangs.
### 🏴‍☠️ District 2: Subterranean Underbelly (Bandit Hideouts)
 * **The Vibe:** Dark, steam-filled drainage tunnels and subterranean hacker dens beneath the street level.
 * **Key Features:**
   * **Gang Turf Wars:** Rival cyber-punks and heavily augmented bandits control the sewer networks.
   * **Infiltration Route:** Since the Millionaire won't let a "dirty kid" through his front lobby, these tunnels serve as the back door into the Skyscraper’s maintenance shafts.
### 🏰 District 3: Platinum Plaza & The Skyscraper
 * **The Vibe:** Pristine, hyper-sanitized white marble and chrome architecture guarded by heavy corporate mechs and automated turret towers.
 * **The Millionaire's Tower:** A massive 100-story monolith. The CEO uses the fourth minor **Chaos Beacon** as an infinite power grid for his corporate empire, treating it like a luxury ornament in his top-floor penthouse.
 * **Infiltration Phase:** You don't take the elevator. You grapple up the exterior glass, fight through laser security floors, and crash his private penthouse party to execute him and take the Beacon!
### 🎯 Alice’s Introduction: "The Red Laser"
As you exit the Skyscraper onto the high-altitude skybridges holding the glowing Chaos Beacon in hand, Alice makes her entrance without saying a single word.
> **[⚠️ WARNING: HIGH-VELOCITY THREAT DETECTED]**
> *Your heavy Dark Armour violently shudders on your shoulders. A thin, blood-red laser line suddenly locks dead-center onto your forehead.*

 * **The Reaction:** Your "spider-sense" (Armour warning) triggers! You snap your head aside just as a high-caliber plasma sniper round pierces the steel wall behind you with a deafening crack.
 * **Who She Is:** Alice is the elite bounty hunter and combat medic of Lampros’ trinity (alongside Wignus and Prince Mike). She doesn't fight out of personal rage—she's a professional executioner acting on divine orders to neutralize the "corrupting dark vessel."
### ⚡ District 4: The Rooftop Skybridges (Alice's Cyber Battlefield)
After her sniper opening, Alice turns the floating neon billboards, rain-slicked skybridges, and rooftop helipads into a tactical, multi-stage sniper arena!
 * **Phase 1: Sniper Line-of-Sight:** You must move between glowing holographic billboards and HVAC units while Alice snipes from elevated perches across the skyline.
 * **Phase 2: The Healer-Bounty Hunter:** When you close the distance, she uses high-mobility grapple boots, shock traps, and self-healing nano-drones to keep her distance while calling down orbital target strikes.
