# Chapter 13: From The Beginning 
### 🎭 The Puppet's Reveal
Sebastian’s giant silhouette form violently collapses back into the 3D theater arena, panting and covered in ink. He looks up, his posture suddenly losing all tension. His regular voice speaks without an ounce of fear:

"Hmph... not bad. You actually passed the test. I figured you'd be a lot dumber than that." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Sebastian The Shadow Speaker\|Sebastian The Shadow Speaker]]


*"What?"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*"Too bad you aren't fighting a real person. Because I am actually..."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Sebastian The Shadow Speaker\|Sebastian The Shadow Speaker]]

Suddenly, Sebastian's head snaps back at an unnatural, mechanical angle. When his mouth opens again, the voice that comes out is high-pitched, musical, and dripping with playful malice—completely replacing his old tone.

"A puppet, ahaha!!!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Poppy The Puppeteer\|Poppy The Puppeteer]]
*(Still using Sebastian's body)*:
"Congratulations, sucker! You survived the opening act. Now follow me. The director wants a word." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Poppy The Puppeteer\|Poppy The Puppeteer]]
Thin, glowing violet strings suddenly pull taut from the dark ceiling grid, lifting Sebastian’s limbs. His dark, smoky form violently shifts and morphs, twisting into the shape of a rigid Royal Security Guard, the joints on his wrists and elbows visibly wooden and segmented.
The Guard woodenly walks ahead, gesturing for you to follow him down a dark corridor that leads deep beneath the amphitheater, finally pushing open two massive double doors.
### 🏛️ The Theatre of Origins
You enter a sprawling, velvet-lined underground theater. The rows are packed with hundreds of figures sitting completely stiff and upright in the dim light. Their posture feels uncannily weird, but you don't have time to inspect them.

 *"Your seat is row 1, spot 1. The premium seat. Don't look away."* -Guard?

You take your seat right at the front. The person sitting directly next to you bounces rigidly in their chair, speaking in an overly enthusiastic, synthetic cadence:

 *"Oh jeez, the show is about to start! I've never been so excited!!!"* -Randy 2
 
A massive, glowing title card appears floating above the stage in elegant, glowing text: **THEATRE OF ORIGINS**.
The spotlight hits the center stage. Out steps **Septer**—the very same companion from Super Fun Land. He looks completely identical, but his eyes are glassy and vacant.

 *"Hello! Good day to everyone. The show is about to start. We have everything ready, but for one thing... We're missing an actor. The main actor. Would anyone please come up to the stage and take their place?"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Septer The Sceptre\|Septer The Sceptre]]
 
A wave of perfectly synchronized whispers ripples through the crowd. Nobody moves. Nobody even blinks. (Because they are all puppets, the choice is already pre-planned). The game pauses, prompting you to stand up and walk onto the stage. The moment your boots hit the floorboards, Septer claps his wooden hands.

*"We found a replacement! Thank you so much to all for coming, and I hope you have a wonderful... Adventure!"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Septer The Sceptre\|Septer The Sceptre]]

### 📜 Act I: The Cosmos of Lampros & Skotos
The heavy crimson curtains violently snap open. The stage background shifts into a beautifully crafted, mechanical shadow-box display representing the cosmos.
SEPTER *(Narrating as the backdrop moves)*:
*"Once upon a time, there was nothing. Just an endless void. It was all empty, but for two beings.*
*One, a being of pure goodness and light: **Lampros**.*
*The other, an entity powered by darkness and hate: **Skotos**.*
*Their eternal clash brought life to the universe, the whole cosmos being created by their rivalry.*
 
(On stage, two massive mechanical puppets—one radiant and golden, the other a swirling mass of black ink—clash together, causing miniature wooden stars to ignite across the set.)

 *One day, the battle grown old, Lampros sought to make a peace treaty with Skotos. But Skotos saw this as an opportunity and struck at Lampros' weakness.*
*Little did Skotos know... it was actually a trap!*

The golden puppet of Lampros splits open, revealing dozens of smaller wizard and knight puppets hiding inside the scenery.
*When he tried to smash Lampros to pieces, Skotos was surprised to find all of Lampros' servants ready to cast a spell. A spell that would lock Skotos within physical objects, scattering his essence across a set of fighting equipment that belonged to an old warrior! After having defeated Skotos, Lampros entered an eternal slumber state, turning into a giant Chaos Beacon...*

(The stage hands pull a lever, and the Skotos puppet is violently dragged apart, its pieces forced into a tiny wooden book, a sword, and a set of armor. A massive, glowing replica of a Chaos Beacon rises from the center stage floor.)

(The music suddenly cuts to a low, droning, unsettling chord. The Septer puppet turns its wooden head 180 degrees, staring directly into your character's eyes.)

*But the rumors say... that someone has brought Skotos back to life. A kid has found the locker where Skotos was sealed, and is now working with him to restore his power...*
*But those are just rumors... Right?"*

### ⚡ THE PLAYBACK GAUNTLET
The moment the line drops, the theater stage violently collapses beneath your feet. You plunge into a surreal, fast-forward chronological nightmare.
The game forces you into a high-speed **"Speedrun Gauntlet"**—a glitchy, hyper-paced replay of your entire journey so far. The background shifts at breakneck speed as you are forced to run through compressed, highly difficult versions of every major milestone you've cleared:
 1. **The Village & Fields:** Dash through a chaotic, fast-forward version of the start where you first found the Tome.
 2. **The Pirate Docks:** Grapple across rapidly collapsing docks while dodging a barrage of cannonballs.
 3. **The Witch’s Curse Dimension:** Navigate a tight, shifting maze of deep forest thorns.
 4. **Lyze HQ & Super Fun Land:** Outrun a giant roller-coaster track collapsing right behind you.
 5. **Wignus Tower:** Scale a vertical gauntlet of moving magic platforms under a strict time limit.
The music scales up in intensity, remixing all the past area themes into a chaotic, overlapping track. Finally, you burst through a literal wooden curtain at the end of the speedrun track, crashing right back onto the theater stage floor.
## 🕷️ BOSS BATTLE: Poppy The Puppeteer
The stage lights flare a violent, mocking purple. Hovering above you in the rafters is **Poppy**. She is wrapped tight in heavy, clanking iron chains that trace back up to the ceiling grid like macabre puppet strings. She glares down with multiple glowing eyes, laughing sharply.
"A spectacular performance! But every tragedy needs a final bow, darling. Let's see how well you dance when I'm the one pulling the strings!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Poppy The Puppeteer\|Poppy The Puppeteer]]
### ⚔️ MECHANICS: The Puppet Master's Ensemble
 * **Phase 1 (The Swarm):** Poppy stays in the rafters, manipulating the battlefield. She fires iron chains downward while summoning waves of wooden puppets that perfectly imitate the lesser enemies you've faced (Shadow Goblins, Raiders, and Docks Slimes). They drop no souls—they are just empty wood designed to overwhelm you.
 * **Phase 2 (The Mid-Show Boss):** When her HP hits 50%, Poppy snarls and drops a massive, heavily armored boss puppet into the center of the arena (a wood-carved replica of a past major threat). While you deal with this heavy-hitter, Poppy rains down tracking projectile strings from above.
With a final, shattering blow, you destroy the giant puppet and use your grappling hook to yank Poppy out of the rafters, slamming her onto the stage floor. Your Dark Sword pierces her core.

The heavy iron chains binding her wrists and limbs rattle violently, but instead of snapping, they pull taut, suspending her broken form like a discarded doll. She doesn't scream or beg. She just lifts her head, her multiple glowing eyes locking dead onto yours as a cold, knowing smile spreads across her face.

"I may be a puppet because these metal chains tie me to my body... But I'm a free puppet. What about you?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Poppy The Puppeteer\|Poppy The Puppeteer]]

Slowly, rigidly, she raises a wooden finger, pointing directly at the dark, pulsing runes of the curse spreading across your arm.

"Do those strings let *YOU* be free? I wonder who is really pulling them... Teeheehee..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Poppy The Puppeteer\|Poppy The Puppeteer]]

(Her words have triple meaning. You are puppeteered by The Tome, Player and Writer)

Her eyes suddenly go completely dark. Her wooden joints splinter, and her form dissolves into a pile of cold, white ash on the stage floor, leaving the iron chains to clatter uselessly into the dirt.

A single, glowing violet soul rises from her remains. Your soul counter rolls up.

## 🔮 The Second Chaos Beacon
With Poppy defeated, the illusory theater violently dissolves into dust, revealing the true hidden chamber behind the stage.
Hovering in the center of a massive stone dais is the **Second Chaos Beacon**, humming with a deep, pulsating resonance. You approach the altar, raising your left hand. The dark runes on your arm flare to life, violently siphoning the energy out of the structure.
The Beacon shatters into obsidian glass. A blinding shockwave ripples through the cavern, sealing the area in darkness.
### 🌌 Inside the Beacon: The World of Strings
The darkness doesn't fade into game over—instead, your vision blurs as your soul is violently pulled inside the Beacon's core.
When you open your eyes, the perspective has changed. You are trapped in a bleak, monochrome world made entirely of wooden stagecraft and velvet curtains. You look down at your arms. You have been transformed into a wooden dummy. Strands of glowing violet energy—puppet strings—stretch from your wrists, elbows, and head, disappearing into a massive, swirling vortex in the sky.
Everywhere you look, hundreds of wooden puppet citizens shuffle through the streets in perfect, eerie synchronization. Every single one of them is bound by identical strings.
### 🤫 GAMEPLAY: The Stealth Social Protocol
 * **The Rules:** You have exactly **1 HP**. A single misstep means instant destruction.
 * **The Mechanic:** To reach the Grand Palace at the end of the street, you must blend in perfectly with the puppet crowd. When the city bells toll, you must walk. When they stop, you must freeze in a rigid pose.
 * **The Danger:** Royal Guard puppets patrol the sidewalks, their mechanical eyes casting red searchlights. If you run, jump, or turn the wrong way while they are looking, your strings will flash red, and the guards will instantly snap your joints.
You carefully mirror the crowd, stepping to the rhythmic, mechanical ticking of the world, until you finally slip past the last line of guards and push through the towering golden doors of the Grand Palace.
### 👑 The Audience with "The One"
The doors slam shut behind you. Sitting on a massive, over-engineered throne at the end of a long carpet is a towering, regal figure draped in velvet—**The One**. He stares down at you with cold, hollow wooden eyes. Behind him, thousands of strings converge into his fingertips like a massive web.

*"Look at this place. Look at what you’ve done. Why are you pulling the strings of an entire society? Why turn everyone into mindless toys?"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*(He laughs, a hollow, echoing sound of clattering wood)*
*"Mindless? No, darling. I gave them order. Look out those windows. Left to their own devices, people tear each other apart for scraps. But under my strings? They march in perfect harmony. You call it tyranny... I call it a flawless script."* -The One

*"That's not a script. It's a cage."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*"And what is freedom but a messy, unpredictable chaos? Control is absolute power. Without a master to guide the hand, the puppet is just dead weight. And a stray puppet... has no place in my theater."* -The One

The One stands up, descending the throne steps. He reaches out, casually snatching a heavy, broad executioner's axe from a standing Royal Guard. He raises the blade, aiming straight above your head.

*"Let us see how well you dance with your lines cut."* -The One

With a brutal downswing, he slices cleanly through the glowing violet strings binding your wrists and head.
The screen instantly flashes white. But instead of shattering into pieces, the dark runes of the Tome flare to absolute life on your wooden skin. For the first time in this world, you are completely untethered—and your true dark power explodes outward.
### ⚡ THE BEACON’S AWAKENING
The monochrome palace shatters like glass. The environment violently folds back into reality, returning you to the stone cavern dais. The blinding light of the broken beacon condenses into a single, brilliant point floating right in front of your chest, taking the shape of a tiny, cute, floating version of Poppy.
ANCIENT VOICE:
**"You who have conquered this Chaos Beacon... take my power. Become stronger."**

The entity opens its tiny eyes, floating over to hover near your shoulder.
ANCIENT VOICE:
**"This is Puppy. Her Puppeteer abilities will guide you on your dark path. Now go... seek my brothers."**

### 🐾 NEW COMPANION UNLOCKED: Puppy
 * **The Companion:** A tiny, floating doll version of Poppy that assists you in combat using "Puppy-teer" mechanics.
 * **Base Ability (Puppet Reanimation):** Whenever you defeat an enemy, Puppy can attach spectral strings to their corpse, raising them as a wooden puppet ally. By default, these puppets morph into the exact form of any lesser enemy you have previously beaten to fight alongside you.
 * **Ultimate Upgrade (The Puppy-teer):** When fully upgraded, Puppy's strings can shoot outward during live combat, chaining down active elite enemies and temporarily forcing them to turn on their own allies, causing total battlefield chaos.
### 🪓 On the Horizon: The Cave Exit
With **46 Souls** burning in your blade, two Chaos Beacons shattered, and your new companion floating by your side, the path ahead leads out of the subterranean darkness.
As you head toward the light of the cavern exit, the heavy, rhythmic thud of a massive battle axe echoes from the surface.
