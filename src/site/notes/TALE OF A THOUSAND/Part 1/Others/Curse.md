If the marks light up, the host will die within the next 3 minutes... But it doesn't end there. Look at this paragraph... it says you can survive even after they light up. It says: *'A sacrifice must be made.'* ...What does that even mean—" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Peter\|Peter]]
