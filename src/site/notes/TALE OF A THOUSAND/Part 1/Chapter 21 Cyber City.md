# Chapter 21: Cyber City (Part 1 - The Underbelly)
Drenched in flickering neon lights, rain-slicked asphalt, and buzzing holographic advertisements, you step into the sprawling metropolis of **[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Locations/Cyber City\|Cyber City]]**.
Before you can take ten steps past the outer slums, a gang of swift subterranean bandits drops from the overhead steam pipes! In a chaotic, high-speed distraction, one of them clips your belt, yanking **The Dark Tome** right out of its leather holster!
Before you can draw your sword, the bandits vanish into the dark sewer grates.
Without the Tome at your hip, its suffocating, oppressive weight instantly vanishes. Your raw strength drops, but the constant, corrupting whisper inside your skull goes dead silent. For the first time in a long time, your mind feels strangely clear.
### 🤝 An Unexpected Ally
As you pursue the bandits toward the lower district, a familiar figure steps out from the shadows near a neon-lit alleyway—**Mike**.
He immediately reaches for his sword, his eyes locking onto you. But as he steps forward, he pauses. He doesn't feel the terrifying, blood-soaked aura radiating from your body.

"You..." Mike's grip on his hilt slacks slightly. "Where is that sinister energy? What happened to you?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]

"Bandits... they stole something important from me," you reply, your voice lacking its usual cold, lethal edge. -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

Mike stares at you for a long moment, studying your posture. Seeing that you aren't drawing a weapon or lunging at his throat, he lets out a quiet sigh and sheathes his blade.

"I don't know what kind of game you're playing... but I won't let cyber-thieves run wild in this city. I'm coming with you." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]
### 🕵️ INFILTRATION: The Subterranean Hideout
Together with Mike, you slip into the dark, steam-filled drainage tunnels beneath the city streets, navigating past automated security cameras and patrolling gang scouts.
As you crouch behind a row of rusted crates, Mike turns to you, his voice low and serious.

"Back at the docks... and in the forest... you fought like a monster," Mike whispers. "Is that really who you are? Or was it whatever was driving you?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]

Without the Dark Tome poisoning your thoughts, you look down at your hands.

"I... I don't want to kill anymore," you state quietly. -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

Mike looks at you, genuine hope flickering in his eyes. "Hold onto that feeling. Prove it to me today." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]
### ⚔️ BOSS BATTLE: Tengaro, Master of Swordplay
*(1-on-1 Honorable Duel)*
You and Mike push open the heavy vault doors at the center of the hideout, stepping into an ornate, underground training hall.
Standing in the center of the room is the leader of the bandits—**Tengaro**. He wears sleek, lightweight cyber-armor, casually spinning a refined, silver-plated rapier in his hand. He has the calm, arrogant demeanor of an older brother who knows he holds all the cards.

"Well, well... guests in my domain," Tengaro chuckles, flicking his rapier cleanly through the air. "I see you brought the famous vigilante himself." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Tengaro, Master of Swordplay\|Tengaro, Master of Swordplay]]

Mike steps forward, but Tengaro holds up a gloved hand, pointing his blade directly at you.

"I am not one to enjoy an amalgamation of artificial flavours. When I eat, I like to enjoy the simplicity of the item I am eating, it's raw and pure flavour. Swordplay is just like that. We mustn't add any more artificial flavours, isn't the flavour of pure and raw swordplay already the sweetest?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Tengaro, Master of Swordplay\|Tengaro, Master of Swordplay]]

Mike glances at you, stepping back against the wall. "It's your fight. I'll make sure his men don't interfere." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]
### 🤺 MECHANICS: The Pure Fencing Duel
 * **The Combat:** Tengaro uses zero magic, zero guns, and zero gadgets. He fights strictly with lightning-fast lunges, precise rapier thrusts, and high-speed parries. You cannot rely on raw dark power—you must time your dodges and strikes perfectly.
With a precise counter-slash, you disarm Tengaro, sending his rapier clattering across the steel floor! Tengaro stumbles back, holding his chest with a smirk.

"Heh... perhaps I needed more spice..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Tengaro, Master of Swordplay\|Tengaro, Master of Swordplay]]
### 💔 The Betrayal
Tengaro falls to one knee. Lying on a velvet pedestal right behind him is **The Dark Tome**.
You walk over and pick up the leather-bound book. The second your fingers make contact with the cover, a violent, surging wave of dark violet energy erupts through your veins. The whispers violently return, instantly flooding your mind with cold, unyielding malice.

*(The Dark Tome snaps open, its voice dripping with cruel demand)*
"Do not dare hesitate, human! Strike him down! Feed his soul to me!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

Your face goes dark. Raised high, your Dark Sword begins to descend straight toward Tengaro's neck—completely breaking the promise you just made!
**CRACK!**
A high-velocity plasma sniper shot shatters the floor between your boots, sending a shower of sparks into the air and knocking you back!
Hovering up on the high ventilation catwalks is a figure draped in tactical combat gear, her high-caliber sniper rifle locked directly onto your chest—**Alice**.

"I told you you couldn't trust him!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Alice-Aid\|Alice-Aid]]

Mike steps forward, staring at your raised blade in absolute, heartbroken disbelief.

"But... But..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]

"Evacuate them! Now! I'll take care of him!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Alice-Aid\|Alice-Aid]]

Mike snaps out of his shock, turning toward Tengaro and the remaining bandits. "Out the back! GO!"
### 🛡️ SURVIVAL BOSS: Alice (The Bounty Hunter & Healer)
Alice leaps down from the catwalks, landing between you and the fleeing bandits.
> **[⚠️ SURVIVAL TRIAL: SURVIVE 02:00.00]**
> *Alice utilizes advanced nano-technology that instantly overheals any damage you inflict. You CANNOT defeat or knock her down—you must survive her relentless tactical assault until the timer expires!*

### ⚔️ MECHANICS: Tactical Lock-Down
 * **Overheal Grid:** Dealing damage to Alice is useless; her nano-drones immediately restore her health bar to 100%.
 * **The Assault:** She cycles rapidly between high-damage sniper shots from afar, close-range shock traps, and orbital target strikes that rain plasma down across the room.
 * **Your Goal:** Focus entirely on dodging, jetpacking across line-of-sight barriers, and staying alive until Mike finishes evacuating the bandits.
The countdown clock hits **00:00**!
Having successfully bought enough time for Mike and Tengaro to escape through the emergency exits, Alice pulls a flash-grenade from her tactical vest and slams it onto the floor!
A blinding white light fills the room. When the smoke clears, Alice is gone, leaving the hideout completely empty.
### 📞 MISSION BRIEFING: The Platinum Tower
You walk out of the quiet, abandoned bandit hideout and step back into the rain-slicked alleys of Cyber City.

Your communicator buzzes. It's **Lyze**.
*"Hey, test subject! I was monitoring your biometrics—glad to see your heart rate and energy levels stabilized after that weird drop earlier!"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

*(A holographic map of the upper city projects from your wrist)*
*"Good news: my long-range scanners just pinpointed the location of the fourth minor **Chaos Beacon**! It's sitting right at the top floor of the **Platinum Skyscraper** in District 3. The building is owned by a crazy wealthy corporate CEO who uses the beacon's energy to power his private tower. Get up there, breach security, and secure that beacon!"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

You close the transmission, looking up through the neon fog toward the towering monolith of the Platinum Skyscraper piercing the clouds...

# Chapter 21: Cyber City (Part 2 - The Skyscraper Ascent)
Stepping into the massive glass lobby of the **Platinum Skyscraper**, the towering atrium glimmers with polished white marble, holographic fish swimming through the air, and pristine chrome elevators.
High above, heavy corporate security drones hum in rhythmic patrol patterns. The front gates snap shut, locking you inside the monolith.

*(The Dark Tome snaps open at your hip, its pages rustling with dark anticipation)*
"A fortress of glass and coin... delicious. The air up here is ripe with weak, pampered souls. How shall we dismantle them, human?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]
### 🎮 PLAYSTYLE CHOICE: Stealth vs. Rampage
The Skyscraper gives you total operational freedom in how you tackle its 100 floors:
 * **🥷 THE PHANTAM ROUTE (Sneak):**
   * Navigate ventilation shafts, shadow corners, and blind spots of security cameras.
   * Disable security rooms to shut down sector turrets.
   * Perform silent stealth-assassinations on patrolling corporate guards to swipe their floor keycards without raising the building-wide alarm.
 * **💥 THE RAMPAGE ROUTE (Heavy Assault):**
   * Draw the Dark Sword and smash through everything in sight!
   * **The Lock-Down Rule:** Security blast doors are magnetically sealed by corporate AI. The Dark Sword refuses to cut through them until **every single soul on the floor has been reaped**.
   * **The Industrial Cleaning Kart:** On designated maintenance floors, you can hijack a heavy-duty, rocket-propelled Industrial Cleaning Kart, plowing through squads of security forces at breakneck speeds!
### 🚧 MID-BOSS: CW (The Construction Worker)
Ascending through the 50th floor, you step into a bizarre, massive testing chamber—a high-tech, holographic room completely simulating a busy downtown city street, complete with asphalt, traffic cones, and detour signs.
Standing in the center of the artificial road is a hulking, simple-minded cyborg in a neon yellow high-vis vest, revving a massive, rocket-powered jackhammer—**CW**.
> **👾 DEV NOTE:**
> CW's design and speech are heavily inspired by **Mr. Bull** from *Peppa Pig*!
> 
"WE'RE DIGGING UP THE ROAD! NO UNSCHEDULED PEDESTRIANS ON MY WATCH! DESTROY! WRECK! SMASH!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/CW, The Construction Worker\|CW, The Construction Worker]]
### ⚔️ ROUTE-SPECIFIC COMBAT:
 * **🥷 Sneak Route:** CW is remarkably dense. While he rambles angrily at a holographic traffic cone, you can slip directly behind his heavy frame and execute a swift, silent backstab to harvest his soul instantly.
 * **💥 Rampage Route:** CW slams his jackhammer into the floor, sending seismic shockwaves through the asphalt! He calls in external corporate demolition cranes that smash their wrecking balls straight through the skyscraper's glass windows! Though the high-tech building immediately self-rebuilds its nanotech glass, those swinging cranes pack a devastating punch!
You drive your blade through CW's core, causing his jackhammer to explode into sparks. His soul surges directly into your hilt!
### 🏗️ THE CRANE GAUNTLET & THE HELIPAD
If your presence was alerted on the lower floors, the remaining construction crane operators outside keep up a relentless barrage, firing heavy plasma charges and swinging steel beams through the upper-floor windows as you fight through squads of elite corporate guards.
Reaching the 99th floor, you find the grand double doors to the CEO’s Executive Penthouse sealed behind a level-5 biometrics lock.
Through the tinted glass of an observation window, you spot an elite corporate officer carrying the **Executive Master Keycard** stepping into a private express elevator heading straight for the rooftop.

"He's heading for the helipad. We cut him off at the top." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]
### 🎆 ROOFTOP BOSS: Ignis, The Pyrotechnic Specialist
You push open the heavy access door, stepping out onto the windswept, rain-slicked helipad at the very peak of the skyscraper. The neon lights of Cyber City spread out for miles below.
*(This encounter CANNOT be snuck!)*
As you creep across the wet metal platform toward the keycard carrier, your boot accidentally crushes a live mortar fuse buried in a crate of corporate celebration supplies.
*WHZZZZ-BANG!*
A blinding red firework rockets straight into the night sky, illuminating the entire rooftop!
Standing atop a supply container is the CEO’s chief event engineer—**Ignis, The Pyrotechnic Specialist**. He wears a blackened hazard mask and wields a multi-barreled launcher packed with military-grade fireworks.

"INTRUDERS ON THE LAUNCH PAD?! Enjoy the grand finale, kid!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Ignis, The Pyrotechnic Specialist\|Ignis, The Pyrotechnic Specialist]]
### ⚔️ MECHANICS: Blinding Pyrotechnics
 * **Flash-Dazzle Mortars:** Ignis fires blinding magnesium rockets that coat your screen in bright white light, forcing you to rely on audio cues and the Dark Armour's shuddering prompts to dodge incoming strikes.
 * **Cluster Fireworks:** He launches high-arching firework clusters that rain explosive embers down across the helipad while remaining crane operators attempt to swipe you off the roof edge.
With a swift dash using your Jetpack, you bypass his pyrotechnic barrage and cleave his launcher in half! Ignis stumbles backward into his mortar crates, his suit igniting in a brief, spectacular display of colorful sparks before dissolving into gray ash.
His purple soul flies directly into the Dark Sword! From his charred gloves, you scoop up the glowing **Executive Master Keycard**.
### 🚪 AT THE CEO'S DOORSTEP
You take the private helipad elevator back down one level, stepping out into the quiet, carpeted hallway of the executive floor.
Standing at the end of the corridor are the colossal, double-gilded gold doors leading directly into the CEO's private Penthouse Suite—where the fourth minor **Chaos Beacon** powers the tower.
Inside, you can hear the faint, frantic pacing of the billionaire CEO... and the heavy, metallic footsteps of his elite personal **Bodyguard**.
You slide the Executive Master Keycard into the lock. The golden doors let out a deep, pneumatic hiss as the lock turns green...

# Chapter 21: Cyber City (Part 3 - Slaves of the Screen)
### 💎 THE PENTHOUSE: The CEO & The Bodyguard
The golden elevator doors slide open with a silent hiss, revealing a sprawling, multi-million-dollar penthouse suite. Floor-to-ceiling panoramic glass overlooks the sprawling neon lights of Cyber City.
Standing behind a massive obsidian desk, frantically pouring himself a glass of expensive champagne with trembling hands, is the owner of the tower—**CEO Sterling**.

"Y-You actually cleared security?! How?! That armor... you're that unsanctioned psycho from the lower districts!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/CEO Sterling\|CEO Sterling]]

*(The Dark Tome snaps open, its pages burning with violet flames)*
"Look at him. Weak, bloated on wealth, hiding behind glass while his city rots below. End him, human." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

You take a step forward, drawing the Dark Sword. CEO Sterling stumbles back, hitting a panic button on his desk.

"APEX! REDACT THIS TRESPASSER IMMEDIATELY!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/CEO Sterling\|CEO Sterling]]

From the shadows behind the desk, a towering silhouette steps into the ambient neon light. Draped in high-density matte-black chrome armor and wielding a pair of high-frequency energy dual-tonfas, his elite personal enforcer steps forward—**Apex, The Executive Guard**.

*"Target locked. Threat level: Catastrophic. Executing permanent termination protocol."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Apex, The Executive Guard\|Apex, The Executive Guard]]
### ⚔️ BOSS BATTLE: Apex, The Executive Guard
*(CEO Sterling cowers behind bulletproof forcefields screaming orders while Apex handles the fight)*
### ⚔️ MECHANICS: High-Frequency Cyber-Combat
 * **Phase 1 (Chrome Counter):** Apex uses lightning-fast parry stances. Hitting his guard reflects kinetic energy directly back at you as an EMP shockwave.
 * **Phase 2 (Overdrive Thrusters):** Apex activates jet-assisted dashes, leaving thermal trails across the Penthouse while launching blinding flash-bang strikes and heavy grapple-swings.
With a crushing counter-strike, you shatter Apex's dual energy blades and plunge your Dark Sword directly through his chrome chest plate! Apex freezes, his visor cracking as his systems shut down. He collapses onto the marble floor, his soul floating into your blade.
CEO Sterling drops his champagne glass, backed up against the outer window, weeping frantically.

"Wait! Please! I have billions! Offshore accounts! Pure gold! Anything you want—!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/CEO Sterling\|CEO Sterling]]

*(Coldly, stepping over Apex's body)*
"I want the Beacon." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]
You drive your sword through his chest without hesitation. His purple soul surges directly into your hilt.
 * **Loot Acquired:** You pick up the thirteenth [[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Others/PageStone\|PageStone]], which was on his desk
### 🌐 INSIDE THE 4TH MINOR BEACON: The Digital Matrix
You walk up to the glowing **Neon Chaos Beacon** floating above CEO Sterling's desk. The moment your hand touches the crystal, your physical body vanishes, and your soul is pulled directly into the Beacon's inner core.
You materialize in a retro, synthwave digital grid—a monochrome world of flickering CRT monitors, streaming neon code, and floating terminal nodes.
> **[⚠️ BEACON TRIAL INITIATED: BYPASS THE FIREWALL GRID]**
### 💻 GAMEPLAY: The Retro Cyber-Bypass
 * **The Challenge:** A fast-paced, digital hacking runner. You must sprint along glowing code-rails, dodging firewall laser barriers, recalibrating encryption terminals, and matching glitch-frequency inputs to navigate through the mainframe!
### 📜 THE LORE ARCHIVES: Slaves of the Screen
As you hack deeper into the mainframe, holographic memory archives begin to project across the digital skyline, revealing the true origin of Cyber City:
> **[ARCHIVE LOG 01: THE DARKNESS]**
> Decades ago, Cyber City was nothing but a cold, impoverished slum starving in total darkness. People fought over scraps in the dirt, desperate for warmth.
> **[ARCHIVE LOG 02: THE FREE POWER]**
> CEO Sterling discovered the Fourth Minor Beacon. Instead of hoarding it in secret, he built the first Tower and gave the city **free, infinite electricity**... on one small condition. Every home had to install a Central Display Screen.
> **[ARCHIVE LOG 03: THE ADDICTION]**
> At first, the screens provided light and news. Then, endless entertainment. Soon, citizens stopped leaving their homes. They stopped looking at each other. They glued their eyes to the glowing blue glass 24 hours a day, eating, sleeping, and living through the feeds.
> **[ARCHIVE LOG 04: THE DIGITAL SLARES]**
> CEO Sterling didn't need an army or guards to rule the city. He controlled what they saw, what they thought, and what they bought. The people willingly traded their freedom for digital comfort.

*(Standing in the digital archive, you realize the horrifying parallel)*
"Poppy... 'The One'... he used wooden strings to turn people into puppets," you whisper, watching the memory of millions of citizens staring blankly into glowing screens. "Sterling didn't need strings. He used **cables and screens**."

*(The Dark Tome chuckles darkly in your mind)*
"Strings on wrists... light on eyes. Humans are so eager to let someone else pull their lines. In the end, a puppet is still a puppet." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]
### ⚡ THE BEACON’S AWAKENING & PET UNLOCK
The synthwave matrix shatters like glass, condensing into a single, glowing neon point of light.

ANCIENT VOICE:
**"You who have conquered this Chaos Beacon... take my power. Become stronger."**

The light takes the form of a floating, retro CRT television drone with expressive, pixelated eyes.

ANCIENT VOICE:
**"This is Pixel. His digital warfare systems will guide you on your dark path. Now go... seek my older brother."**
> **🤖 NEW COMPANION: Pixel**
>  * **Appearance:** A floating, retro-styled neon screen drone with animated pixel faces (happy, angry, glitched) and dual floating laser emitters.
>  * **Base Ability (Glitch Pulse):** Emits a digital EMP wave that scrambles enemy HUDs, stuns robotic enemies, and forces organic foes to attack holographic decoys.
>  * **Ultimate Upgrade (Overclock Matrix):** Fires an orbital data beam that turns surrounding enemy projectiles into bonus energy shields for you while boosting your attack speed.
### 🌆 EXITING THE SKYSCRAPER
Your soul snaps back into reality. You open your eyes, standing in the silent Penthouse over CEO Sterling's body. The Fourth Minor Chaos Beacon is yours.

*(Your communicator buzzes. It's Lyze)*
*"Incredible work, test subject! I just picked up the signal drop—the fourth minor beacon's grid is completely offline! That just leaves the **Central Chaos Beacon** inside the capital!"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

*(Her voice gets slightly quieter, hesitant)*
*"Hey... your biometric readings during that fight... they were completely cold. Zero spike in adrenaline, zero fear. It's like you didn't even feel like you were fighting a person... Be careful out there, alright?"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

You shut off the communicator without replying. You step out onto the high-altitude skybridge connecting the top of the skyscraper to the long, deserted causeway leading out of the city.
The rain pours over your heavy dark armor. Ahead lies the long, quiet road to the capital...

# Chapter 21: Cyber City (Part 3 - The Leap & The Trinity)
### 🪶 THE LEAP OF FAITH
You step out onto the rain-slicked ledge of the 100th-floor skybridge. Below you, Cyber City is a sea of flickering neon, howling wind, and miles of empty drop.

*(The Dark Tome snaps open frantically at your hip, its pages fluttering in the storm)*
"WHAT ARE YOU—YOU'RE NOT GOING TO—" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

Without a single word, you step off the edge.
You plunge straight into the open sky.

*"CRAZY HUMAN! IT'S YOUR DOOM! WE'RE GOING TO SPLATTER ACROSS THE PAVEMENT!"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

The wind roars in your ears. The city skyline blurs into streaks of neon light as terminal velocity locks in. Five seconds to impact. Three seconds. Two seconds.
"Give me wings." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

*"WHY SHOULD I?! I am the ancient entity of Skotos! You do not command—"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

*"DO IT. NOW."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

Your voice cuts through the roaring wind with such terrifying, cold authority that the Tome’s pages violently shudder. Realizing you won't hesitate to let both of you shatter on the asphalt, dark violet energy explodes from your shoulder blades!
Two massive, tattered obsidian wings burst from your dark armor! You flap them once with monstrous strength right above the street level—a shockwave shattering the nearby pavement—and land gracefully on your feet.
*(The Dark Tome falls dead silent in its holster, its dark runes flickering with genuine unease. For the first time since you found it in the village... it realizes the dynamic has flipped. You aren't its puppet anymore. You're using it like a tool.)*
> **[🪶 NEW TRAVERSAL UNLOCKED: DARK WINGS]**
>  * **Movement Overhaul:** You gain a **Double Jump**, drastically increased sprint speed, extended dash distance, and reduced gravity control while airborne.
### 🌉 THE CITY EXIT: The Holy Trinity
With the city’s power grid completely dead and smoke rising from the blacked-out skyscraper behind you, you march down the central highway toward the colossal iron gates leading out of Cyber City.
Standing under the archway, blocking the road to the Capital, are three figures waiting in silence—**Mike**, **Alice**, and **Wignus**.
Mike steps forward, his hand resting on the hilt of his blessed sword. His face is tense, filled with deep anger, but his eyes lack any desire for simple revenge.

"You lied to me," Mike says, his voice echoing over the quiet highway. "In that hideout... you looked me in the eye and said you didn't want to kill anymore. I actually believed you." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]

"Get out of my way, Mike," you answer, your voice hollow. -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

"I'm not doing this for revenge. But I swore an oath to protect these lands, and I won't let a monster walk out those gates. This isn't personal... it's justice." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]

Alice steps up onto a high highway billboard above, racking the bolt of her massive plasma sniper rifle with a cold, metallic click.

"Save the speech, Mike. He’s a walking mass-hazard. Threat level is absolute—let's make this quick." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Alice-Aid\|Alice-Aid]]

Wignus leans back against a steel pillar, twirling his glowing wooden staff with a lazy, amused smirk. He glances past you, locking eyes directly with the book on your hip.

"Well, well. Look who grew a pair of dark wings," Wignus chuckles softly, leaning casually on his staff. "Still carrying that ancient trash on your hip, huh? Though last time we fought, it was driving the bus. Now you're the one holding the reins? Man... you really lost your grip, old book." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Wignus The Wizard\|Wignus The Wizard]]

*(The Dark Tome snarls furiously)*
"Silence, spell-caster! Human, slaughter them all!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]
### ⚔️ BOSS BATTLE: The Holy Trinity
*(3-v-1 Tactical Showdown)*
### 👥 THE ENEMY ROLES:
 1. **Mike (The Powerhouse Vanguard):** Aggressive close-range combatant. Uses heavy holy sword slashes, shield bashes, and relentless pressure to keep you from reaching Alice or Wignus.
 2. **Wignus (The Arcane Controller):** Mid-range caster. Lays down lightning traps, gravity displacement zones, and magic shields to disrupt your movement.
 3. **Alice (The Tactical Medic/Sniper):** High-altitude sniper. Cycles between firing armor-piercing plasma rounds at you and high-potency **Nano-Healing Darts** targeted at Mike and Wignus when their HP drops.
### 💡 SPECIAL COMBAT MECHANIC: HEAL STEAL
When Alice locks a green targeting laser onto Mike or Wignus to fire a Nano-Healing Dart, a tactical warning flashes on your HUD.
 * **The Counter:** Use your **Dark Wings** double-jump to dash directly into the path of Alice's healing dart!
 * **The Result:** You **intercept and steal the heal**, restoring your own health bar while leaving Mike completely exposed to your follow-up strike!
### 💥 THE AFTERMATH
With a blinding wing-dash, you steal Alice's final healing dart, intercept Wignus's spell grid, and shatter Mike's guard with a heavy downward slash!
Mike stumbles back, hurt, his sword digging into the asphalt to keep himself upright. Alice drops down from the billboard, landing in front of Mike with a smoke grenade ready, while Wignus casually steps up, tapping his staff on the ground to form a massive protective barrier.

"Tch... the kid's got wings now," Wignus mutters, his lazy smirk finally fading into a serious frown. "Mike, we fall back. Fighting him here with the city grid dead is tactical suicide." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Wignus The Wizard\|Wignus The Wizard]]

Alice throws the smoke grenade!
*THWIP-FLASH!*
A dense wall of white chemical smoke engulfs the highway exit. When your Dark Wings blow the smoke away, the trio has retreated into the hills—leaving the grand gates to the Capital wide open.
You sheath your Dark Sword. The wind blows through your dark wings as you take your first steps onto the long, desolate highway leading toward the final region.
