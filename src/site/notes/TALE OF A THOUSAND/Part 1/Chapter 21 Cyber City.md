# Chapter 21: Cyber City (Part 1 - The Underbelly)
Drenched in flickering neon lights, rain-slicked asphalt, and buzzing holographic advertisements, you step into the sprawling metropolis of **[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Part 1/Locations/Cyber City\|Cyber City]]**.
Before you can take ten steps past the outer slums, a gang of swift subterranean bandits drops from the overhead steam pipes! In a chaotic, high-speed distraction, one of them clips your belt, yanking **The Dark Tome** right out of its leather holster!
Before you can draw your sword, the bandits vanish into the dark sewer grates.
Without the Tome at your hip, its suffocating, oppressive weight instantly vanishes. Your raw strength drops, but the constant, corrupting whisper inside your skull goes dead silent. For the first time in a long time, your mind feels strangely clear.
### 🤝 An Unexpected Ally
As you pursue the bandits toward the lower district, a familiar figure steps out from the shadows near a neon-lit alleyway—**Mike**.
He immediately reaches for his sword, his eyes locking onto you. But as he steps forward, he pauses. He doesn't feel the terrifying, blood-soaked aura radiating from your body.

"You..." Mike's grip on his hilt slacks slightly. "Where is that sinister energy? What happened to you?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]

"Bandits... they stole something important from me," you reply, your voice lacking its usual cold, lethal edge. -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

Mike stares at you for a long moment, studying your posture. Seeing that you aren't drawing a weapon or lunging at his throat, he lets out a quiet sigh and sheathes his blade.

"I don't know what kind of game you're playing... but I won't let cyber-thieves run wild in this city. I'm coming with you." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]
### 🕵️ INFILTRATION: The Subterranean Hideout
Together with Mike, you slip into the dark, steam-filled drainage tunnels beneath the city streets, navigating past automated security cameras and patrolling gang scouts.
As you crouch behind a row of rusted crates, Mike turns to you, his voice low and serious.

"Back at the docks... and in the forest... you fought like a monster," Mike whispers. "Is that really who you are? Or was it whatever was driving you?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]

Without the Dark Tome poisoning your thoughts, you look down at your hands.

"I... I don't want to kill anymore," you state quietly. -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]

Mike looks at you, genuine hope flickering in his eyes. "Hold onto that feeling. Prove it to me today." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]
### ⚔️ BOSS BATTLE: Tengaro, Master of Swordplay
*(1-on-1 Honorable Duel)*
You and Mike push open the heavy vault doors at the center of the hideout, stepping into an ornate, underground training hall.
Standing in the center of the room is the leader of the bandits—**Tengaro**. He wears sleek, lightweight cyber-armor, casually spinning a refined, silver-plated rapier in his hand. He has the calm, arrogant demeanor of an older brother who knows he holds all the cards.

"Well, well... guests in my domain," Tengaro chuckles, flicking his rapier cleanly through the air. "I see you brought the famous vigilante himself." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Tengaro, Master of Swordplay\|Tengaro, Master of Swordplay]]

Mike steps forward, but Tengaro holds up a gloved hand, pointing his blade directly at you.

"I am not one to enjoy an amalgamation of artificial flavours. When I eat, I like to enjoy the simplicity of the item I am eating, it's raw and pure flavour. Swordplay is just like that. We mustn't add any more artificial flavours, isn't the flavour of pure and raw swordplay already the sweetest?" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Tengaro, Master of Swordplay\|Tengaro, Master of Swordplay]]

Mike glances at you, stepping back against the wall. "It's your fight. I'll make sure his men don't interfere." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]
### 🤺 MECHANICS: The Pure Fencing Duel
 * **The Combat:** Tengaro uses zero magic, zero guns, and zero gadgets. He fights strictly with lightning-fast lunges, precise rapier thrusts, and high-speed parries. You cannot rely on raw dark power—you must time your dodges and strikes perfectly.
With a precise counter-slash, you disarm Tengaro, sending his rapier clattering across the steel floor! Tengaro stumbles back, holding his chest with a smirk.

"Heh... perhaps I needed more spice..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Tengaro, Master of Swordplay\|Tengaro, Master of Swordplay]]
### 💔 The Betrayal
Tengaro falls to one knee. Lying on a velvet pedestal right behind him is **The Dark Tome**.
You walk over and pick up the leather-bound book. The second your fingers make contact with the cover, a violent, surging wave of dark violet energy erupts through your veins. The whispers violently return, instantly flooding your mind with cold, unyielding malice.

*(The Dark Tome snaps open, its voice dripping with cruel demand)*
"Do not dare hesitate, human! Strike him down! Feed his soul to me!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

Your face goes dark. Raised high, your Dark Sword begins to descend straight toward Tengaro's neck—completely breaking the promise you just made!
**CRACK!**
A high-velocity plasma sniper shot shatters the floor between your boots, sending a shower of sparks into the air and knocking you back!
Hovering up on the high ventilation catwalks is a figure draped in tactical combat gear, her high-caliber sniper rifle locked directly onto your chest—**Alice**.

"I told you you couldn't trust him!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Alice-Aid\|Alice-Aid]]

Mike steps forward, staring at your raised blade in absolute, heartbroken disbelief.

"But... But..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mike\|Mike]]

"Evacuate them! Now! I'll take care of him!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Alice-Aid\|Alice-Aid]]

Mike snaps out of his shock, turning toward Tengaro and the remaining bandits. "Out the back! GO!"
### 🛡️ SURVIVAL BOSS: Alice (The Bounty Hunter & Healer)
Alice leaps down from the catwalks, landing between you and the fleeing bandits.
> **[⚠️ SURVIVAL TRIAL: SURVIVE 02:00.00]**
> *Alice utilizes advanced nano-technology that instantly overheals any damage you inflict. You CANNOT defeat or knock her down—you must survive her relentless tactical assault until the timer expires!*

### ⚔️ MECHANICS: Tactical Lock-Down
 * **Overheal Grid:** Dealing damage to Alice is useless; her nano-drones immediately restore her health bar to 100%.
 * **The Assault:** She cycles rapidly between high-damage sniper shots from afar, close-range shock traps, and orbital target strikes that rain plasma down across the room.
 * **Your Goal:** Focus entirely on dodging, jetpacking across line-of-sight barriers, and staying alive until Mike finishes evacuating the bandits.
The countdown clock hits **00:00**!
Having successfully bought enough time for Mike and Tengaro to escape through the emergency exits, Alice pulls a flash-grenade from her tactical vest and slams it onto the floor!
A blinding white light fills the room. When the smoke clears, Alice is gone, leaving the hideout completely empty.
### 📞 MISSION BRIEFING: The Platinum Tower
You walk out of the quiet, abandoned bandit hideout and step back into the rain-slicked alleys of Cyber City.

Your communicator buzzes. It's **Lyze**.
*"Hey, test subject! I was monitoring your biometrics—glad to see your heart rate and energy levels stabilized after that weird drop earlier!"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

*(A holographic map of the upper city projects from your wrist)*
*"Good news: my long-range scanners just pinpointed the location of the fourth minor **Chaos Beacon**! It's sitting right at the top floor of the **Platinum Skyscraper** in District 3. The building is owned by a crazy wealthy corporate CEO who uses the beacon's energy to power his private tower. Get up there, breach security, and secure that beacon!"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Lyze\|Lyze]]

You close the transmission, looking up through the neon fog toward the towering monolith of the Platinum Skyscraper piercing the clouds...
