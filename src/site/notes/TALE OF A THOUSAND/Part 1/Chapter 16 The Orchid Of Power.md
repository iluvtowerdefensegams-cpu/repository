# Chapter 16: The Orchid Of Power
With Salariem's bargain accepted, you step back out of the high-tech warmth of Lyze HQ and venture deep into the pristine, untouched heart of the Dark Forest. The further you trek, the more the regular foliage gives way to strange, twisting roots that pulse with rhythmic energy.
Deep within a hidden, sun-dappled grove, the music of the forest shifts. The low hum of insects transforms into a sharp, beautiful, yet deeply unsettling violin melody. In the center of the grove floats a breathtaking, glowing crimson plant: **The Orchid Of Power**.
Standing directly before it, drawing a bow across a crystalline violin, is a striking woman draped in woven vines and flowers. Her eyes snap open, flashing a dangerous emerald green.

"You reek of rot and old leather, bearer of the Tome. The forest speaks of your path—a trail of ash and shattered souls. I am Mary the Musician, and I will defend the Orchid Of Power with my life! Your discordant noise ends here!" -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mary The Musician\|Mary The Musician]]
### 🎻 BOSS BATTLE: Mary The Musician
### 🎹 MECHANICS: The Tech-Rhythm Duel
The "brutally harder" nature of the game spikes completely here. Your standard sword attacks are entirely useless against Mary's barrier of thorned flowers and toxic roots.
 * **The Setup:** You instantly whip out the tech-instrument (a glowing electronic synthesizer/piano) provided by Lyze. The UI shifts into a high-speed rhythm game track (resembling *Friday Night Funkin'* or *Just Dance* inputs).
 * **The Combat:** Mary plays complex, aggressive violin solos that send rows of sharp, toxic floral notes flying down your screen. Since you are playing on the post-Judgement difficulty, the perfect-input hitboxes are incredibly narrow, and **there is no dodge invulnerability**. You must hit the keys with absolute precision to counter her melodies.
 * **The Punishment:** Missing a sequence doesn't just lower your score; it causes the toxic herbs around your feet to detonate, inflicting a stacking magic wound that chunks your HP. You have to chain perfect "Symphony Combos" together to build a magic bar and blast her barrier apart.
With a final, frantic crescendo, you execute a perfect sequence, hitting a massive chord that sends a surge of distortion through Mary's crystalline violin. The instrument shatters in her hands, and the surrounding thorned barriers violently wither into ash.
Mary falls to her knees, clutching her chest as her life force fades into the forest soil.

"The melody... it's gone... The silence... is deafening..." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Mary The Musician\|Mary The Musician]]

A glowing purple soul rises from her remains, bringing you one step closer to the barrier limit.
### 🎒 Plucking the Orchid
With Mary defeated, the path to the altar is completely clear. You approach the glowing pedestal and pluck the volatile **Orchid Of Power**. The moment the stem snaps, the deep crimson petals flare with a strange, highly concentrated chemical energy, filling your inventory with a strong, aromatic scent.

*The eye on your blade narrows, inspecting the flower.*
"A fascinating specimen, partner. It drips with potential. Let's head back to the merchant before the perfume fades." -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/The Dark Tome\|The Dark Tome]]

You retrace your steps through the shadowed canopies of the forest, the crimson herb tucked safely away. You push open the heavy pneumatic doors of Lyze HQ, the hum of machinery greeting you once more. You walk right up to the inventory counter, standing directly in front of Salariem.
### 🤝 Part 2 of the Deal
You place the glowing Orchid of Power onto the metallic counter. Salariem gaps at it, his usual comedic grin widening as he quickly sweeps the flower into a secure, lead-lined containment box underneath his register.

*"Aha! You actually did it! Marvelous, simply marvelous! But hold your horses, dark savior, because the deal isn't quite done yet. I can't just hand over high-grade military antenna components for raw weeds! I need this turned into a highly refined, stabilized potion first."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Salariem The Shopkeeper\|Salariem The Shopkeeper]]

*"You said you just needed the herb."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/You\|You]]
 
*"Details, details! I'm a shopkeeper, not a chemist! To get this stuff brewed safely, you need to take it to a... let's call him a professional acquaintance of mine. His name is Pike, and he sets up shop deep within the Toxic Swamps at the lowest basin of the forest. Here—you're gonna need this if you want to breathe past his front door."* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Salariem The Shopkeeper\|Salariem The Shopkeeper]]

Salariem reaches under the counter and tosses a heavy, industrial **Gas Mask** at your chest.

*"Pike's a bit... eccentric. Tell him the potion is for me, get the brew, and bring it back here. Do that, and the antenna part is yours. Scout's honor!"* -[[TALE OF A THOUSAND/Characters/Salariem The Shopkeeper\|Salariem The Shopkeeper]]

You strap the gas mask to your belt. The dark runes on your arm flare with a cold impatience, but the path to the mountain remains locked behind this errand. You turn back toward the heavy exit doors, setting your coordinates for the deep, foggy swamplands.
