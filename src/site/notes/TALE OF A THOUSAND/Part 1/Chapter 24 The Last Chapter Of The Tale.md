## Chapter 24: The Last Chapter Of The Tale
### The Last Barrier
The shimmering forcefield towers before the city gates, humming with high-tier defensive magic. Strapped tightly to your waist by heavy, iron-studded leather belts, the dark leather binding of the Dark Tome thumps heavily against your hip with every step—a physical, unyielding burden of pure corruption. It doesn't float; it anchors you to the earth as it hungers for its target. To shatter this final barrier, the Tome demands its **95th Soul**.
### Boss Encounter: Dani the Dragonrider
> *"Everyone is safe. The city is empty. You'll find nothing inside but me!"*

From the storm-wracked clouds above, a shadow swoops down. Dani the Dragonrider circles overhead, her battle-scarred wyrm cutting through the ash. She isn't fighting for power, glory, or a throne—she is the city's last guardian, standing alone in a selfless rear-guard defense to ensure every single citizen had time to evacuate.

[ BOSS ENCOUNTER: DANI THE DRAGONRIDER ]
Phase 1: Aerial Stun (Bow) ➔ Phase 2: Boarding Sequence (Grapple/Jetpack/Wings) ➔ Phase 3: Saddle Duel

#### Encounter Narrative & Mechanics
 * **Phase I: Grounding the Beast**
   The dragon dives, raining down dragonfire across the cobblestones. You draw your **Bow**, aiming for the exposed tendon near the wyrm's neck. The arrow hits true, locking the dragon's jaw and stunning the beast mid-flight, causing it to hover erratically in low altitude.
 * **Phase II: High-Altitude Boarding**
   With the beast momentarily halted, you trigger your **Grappling Hook**, embedding the line directly into the dragon's heavy armor plating. Engaging your **Jetpack** thrusters, you shoot skywards as your **Wings** snap open to control the trajectory, vaulting you straight onto the dragon's back.
 * **Phase III: Saddle Duel**
   High above the battlefield, on the mounting saddle of the thrashing beast, you clash steel with Dani. Her spear strikes with fierce desperation, but the Dark Tome bound to your side pulses with cold light, siphoning her strength. With one final blow, Dani falls—and her soul is torn away to become the **95th Soul** bound within the book.
## ACT I: The Fallen Guardians
### The Gate & The Shadow
The forcefield shatters into blinding dust as the Dark Tome—strapped heavily to your hip by leather belts—pulses with the stolen power of **Dani the Dragonrider**, your 95th soul. You step over the ruined gate and into the legendary city. It is entirely empty; everyone evacuated while Dani bought them time.
### The Warning (Wignus & Alice)
Deep within the silent avenues, standing before the steps of the Central Beacon, two final sentinels block your path: **Wignus** and **Alice**.
> *"You won't step another foot toward the royal chamber,"* Wignus growls, weapon drawn.
> *"He's just a boy!"* Alice screams through tears. *"We won't let you touch the Prince!"*
> 
During the brutal battle, the truth comes light: **Mike**, the simple, kind-hearted boy you met earlier, is **The Prince Of The Tale**. Wignus and Alice fight with desperate, suicidal fury to protect him, but against the power of ninety-seven souls, they fall. You harvest their souls (**96th & 97th**), leaving their bodies on the marble stairs.
## ACT II: The Broken Reunion
### The Last Beacon
You reach the threshold of the Central Beacon. Prince Mike stands alone near the crystal core, but as you approach the corpses of his fallen knights, he speaks into the quiet air.
> *"Even if you killed them... I'm not alone."*
> 
Out of the shadows steps a figure you haven't seen since the beginning of your journey—a man long missing, presumed dead or fled.
**Your Father.**
He stares at you, not with warmth or tearful eyes, but with cold, absolute devastation. Mike has told him everything: the massacres, the dark path, the stolen souls.
> *"My son... I am not proud of what you've become. I must stop you."*
> 
Mike steps forward to fight alongside him, but your father raises a hand, holding an ancient weapon emitting blinding light: **Lampros' Blade**.
> *"No, Prince Mike. Stay back,"* your father demands coldly. *"This is my child. Let me fix my mistake."*

[ BOSS ENCOUNTER: THE FATHER ]
Special Condition: High Weapon Damage (Lampros' Blade) / Zero Combat Skill
Strategy: Easy punish mechanics; raw power undermined by sloppy footwork.

### The Unforgivable Strike
Despite wielding an ancient relic of pure light, your father has no true combat experience. You dodge his clumsy swings and strike him down without hesitation. As your father falls, his soul is ripped into the Tome (**98th Soul**).
Mike gasps in horror, rushing to the dying man's side. He looks up at you, eyes wide as he realizes the sheer monster standing before him—a killer who wouldn't even hesitate to butcher his own blood.
Grief turns to righteous fury. Mike picks up **Lampros' Blade** from your father's lifeless hand. Blinding **Light Wings** burst from Mike's back, burning bright against your dark wings.
## ACT III: Prince Mike (The Prince of the Tale)
[ BOSS ENCOUNTER: PRINCE MIKE ]
Theme: Light (Lampros) vs. Darkness (Skotos)
Arena: Cosmic Reality Distortion (The primordial battleground of the Gods)

### Intro Phase
> *"I really hoped we didn't have to get to this point. I hoped you could be a good person... But I can see you're not. I can't fail now. Everyone is counting on me! Wignus... Alice... your own father... If I let you win, they will all have died for nothing! I MUST FIGHT... FOR THEIR LEGACY!"*
> 
### Phase 2 Transition
When Mike's health drops to half, the sky splits open. The city around you dissolves into a swirling cosmic vacuum—the ancient background of **Skotos and Lampros** locked in primordial conflict.
> *"Feeling like you're gonna be good now? No? ...Alright then. I HOPE YOU DON'T MIND IF I DON'T HOLD BACK!"*
> 
## ACT IV: The Crossroads of Fate
Upon depleting Prince Mike's health bar, the cosmic arena fades. Mike collapses to his knees, bleeding, yet his eyes hold a soft, lingering pity that stirs a sudden, sharp pang of regret deep in your chest.
### Choice Prompt: [ KILL ] or [ SPARE ]
#### Path A: [ KILL ]
You step forward and deliver a final, decisive slash across his chest. Mike doesn't block. He simply closes his eyes and accepts it, falling limp as his soul becomes the **99th Soul**.
#### Path B: [ SPARE ]
You lower your weapon and extend a hand to help him up. The Tome takes possession over you and delivers the final strike. But Mike sneers faintly, shifting back with a swift dodge.
> *"Heh, did you really think that after all those times you've betrayed me, I would—"*
> 
Before he can finish, a jagged spike of dark energy bursts straight through his chest from behind, striking from far outside weapon range. The Dark Tome takes physical form behind him, pulling its shadowy blade back as Mike collapses dead. (**99th Soul**).
## ACT V: The Tome's Betrayal
With 99 souls collected, the heavy book unclasps itself from your leather belt for the first time in the entire game. It floats into the air alongside the dark sword. A towering shadowy manifestation—**The Dark Puppet**—materializes behind it.
> **Tome:** *"99 souls, partner. Do you know what that means? You failed."*
 **You:** *"Lyze, Salariem, Steve, Brandon... There were so many souls I could've taken..."*
> **You:** *"Don't worry... YOURS WILL SUFFICE!"* 

[ TRUE FINAL BOSS: THE DARK PUPPET ]
Condition: Curse Timer Active
Difficulty: Extreme (Hardest encounter in the game)
### Outcome A: Player Defeated / Timer Expires
The Dark Puppet impales you. Your vision fades into black as your soul is yanked into the pages of the Tome.
### Outcome B: Player Defeats The Dark Puppet (Special Victory)
If you manage to beat the Dark Puppet before the curse timer runs out:
 * **Rewards Unlocked:**
   * Exclusive **Pagestone?** material drop (for part 2) (dropped from a page torn out during the struggle).
   * Unlocks **Skotos Difficulty** for Chapter 1 on New Game+.
   * Unlocks secret boss **Tyme**.
   * Early Access content for Part 2.
> **Dark Puppet:** *"Heh... You think you beat my puppet? But you forgot something..."*
The heavy dark armor suddenly slams onto your body. It tightens instantly, crushing your bones and pulverizing your body into dust.
## Epilogue: The Darkness Falls
The Dark Tome claims your soul—the **100th Soul**.
The empty, heavy armor floats onto the shadowy Puppet, sealing the dark entity in physical iron. Moving with your stolen strength, the armored Puppet turns to the Central Beacon, where the soul of **Lampros** rests within the crystal core.
With one brutal strike, the Puppet shatters the beacon.
The crystal explodes into dying embers. A wave of absolute darkness ripples outward across the horizon, smothering the sun and plunging the entire land into eternal night. Hope is completely extinguished...

*...But somewhere, in a distant region nearby, there might just be someone left with the power to fight the darkness.*
*That, however, will be another tale.*
## Post-Credits Scene
The dark screen holds for several seconds. Suddenly, the sound of someone gasping for air cuts through the quiet.
The camera snaps to a bedroom. **The Writer / Narrator** bolts upright in bed, drenched in sweat.
> *"OH!"*
> 
He breathes heavily, clutching his chest, looking around his normal room. He lets out a long sigh and buries his face in his hands.
> *"Whew... It was all just a dream... Or was it?"*
> 
The camera slowly pans down to the floor beside his bed. Hidden in the shadows beneath his nightstand rests **a Tome**, glowing with a faint, crimson light.
The screen cuts to black instantly.
