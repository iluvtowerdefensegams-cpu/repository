# Introduction
Once upon a time there was nothing. Just an endless void. It was all empty, but for two beings.
One, a being of pure goodness and light, Lampros (Brilliant/Radiant in Greek)
The other, an entity powered by darkness and hate, Skotos (Darkness in Greek)
Their eternal clash brought life to the universe, the whole cosmos being created by their rivalry
--story of how the dark one lost and fragments of soul were sealed within a tome, a sword and an armour and how it possessed a human to do it's will and how it broke free (reached central beacon, reforged itself, reborn anew)
--light one entered eternal slumber scattering into the beacons (when the Tome reached central one, the dark one broke the light one's soul and used it's essence to be reborn)
--light one's soul must be restored or else dark one will forever reign the cosmos
--mentions of a legendary being superior to both (the writer) who barely appears in part 1 and 2, and has main plot in part 3 (though he's the cause of all this)
