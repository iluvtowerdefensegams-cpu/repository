The map-wide genocide sweep in Chapter 23—just as Lyze figures out what you are doing and tries to lock down the TP grid—is a terrifyingly hype setup, and "The Long Walk" with the Tome's unyielding, evil confidence sets up the endgame tension perfectly.
Here is the finalized blueprint for the remaining chapters of Part 1:
## 🗺️ Part 1 Endgame Blueprint
### 🏙️ Chapter 21: Cyber City
 * **The Scope:** A massive, feature-packed metropolis hub with multi-part side quests, high-tech hazards, and the fifth/final minor Chaos Beacon.
 * **Goals:** Collect PageStones #13, #14, and #15, harvest souls, get the final minor Beacon companion, and gear up for the endgame.
### 🚶 Chapter 22: The Long Walk
 * **The Vibe:** High-tension traversal along the causeway leading toward The City.
 * **The Dialogue:** A quiet, ominous conversation between **You** and **The Tome** (inspired by *Undertale Genocide*). You test his authority (*"What if you're the one standing in my path?"*), and he responds with cold, smiling arrogance (*"Well, we'll have to see once we claim the last beacon."*).
### 🧹 Chapter 23: Final Rush / The Sweep
 * **The Barrier:** Reaching The City gates reveals a barrier requiring **95 Souls** to shatter.
 * **The Lockdown:** Lyze realizes your true intention and scrambles to shut down the Teleportation (TP) network, but you squeeze through a node just before she cuts the power.
 * **The Map Purge:** You back-track through the entire world, systematically cleansing every region you've ever visited of all remaining life:
   Village → Green Fields → Pirate Docks → Beach Shore → Dark Forest → HQ → Pike Swamp → Super Fun Land → Wignus Tower → Dark Caves → Theater Of Origins → Mountain → Dark Forest → Green Fields → Ashland Desert → Nuclear Factory → Cyber City → The City
 * **The Gatekeeper Boss:** With 95+ souls, you shatter the barrier and fight **Dani the Dragonrider** (female dragonrider) to gain entry into the capital.
### ⚔️ Chapter 24: The Final Reckoning
 * **The Target:** Reaching the 100th Soul.
 * **The Execution List:** Confronting and harvesting the central figures of the world: **Wignus**, **Alice**, **Dad**, and **Mike**.
 * **The 100th Soul:** Breaking the Tome's seal once and for all.
### 📜 Chapter 25: Epilogue
 * **The Aftermath:** The conclusion of Part 1 and setup for what comes next.
