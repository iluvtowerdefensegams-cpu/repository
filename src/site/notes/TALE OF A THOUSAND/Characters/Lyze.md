# Lyze (Analyze)
# Role: 
The Mentor / The Ultimate Survivor / Chief of Tech HQ
# Visual Appearance: 
Lyze looks like a chaotic, brilliant young inventor. She wears a slightly oversized, tech-lined lab coat covered in grease smudges, tool belts slung across her waist, and glowing holographic bracers on her forearms. She usually has a pair of digital welding goggles pushed up onto her forehead.
# Personality & Philosophy:
Lyze is completely driven by logic, curiosity, and the thrill of creation. Unlike Mike, who fights for moral righteousness, Lyze views the entire world—including the player's terrifying curse—as a fascinating mathematical equation waiting to be solved. She is energetic, fast-talking, and entirely unfazed by the dark, spooky atmosphere of the forest or the Tome's menacing presence. To her, a soul-eating demonic book isn't a holy terror; it's just "highly volatile bio-organic energy."
She treats the player like a highly valued research partner (or a very interesting guinea pig), providing them with gadgets like the Grappling Hook and Jetpack simply because she wants to see how far human limits can be pushed. Because she relies entirely on machines, logic, and distance, she is one of the only characters the player cannot successfully kill when the dark route takes over. She is too smart to get caught, making her the ultimate tactical brains behind the global evacuation effort in Part 2.
